South Carolina Department of Motor Vehicles 

 

 

 

Instructions for Completing 
Court Order Restriction of Driving Privileges 
Tobacco Prevention Act - Minor 
These  instructions  are  being  provided  to  help  the  court  complete  Form  VS-200.    The  document  must  be 
completed in its entirety for the Driver Records Office to process it.  This document should only be used to 
impose driving restrictions under Section 16-17-500 (E)-(4).  The information requested should be typed or 
printed clearly.  If the information requested is not provided, Driver Records will return the document to the 
court for completion. 

VS-200 (IS) 
(Est.10/06) 

 

ection 1 – Judge’s Name 

This section identifies the judge who is requesting DMV to restrict the minor’s driving privileges. 

ection 2 – Minor’s Information 

This section must be completed in its entirety.  List the minor’s name, address, city, state, zip code, driver’s 
license or beginner’s permit number, and date of birth.  Also list the legal guardian’s name, address, city, 
state and zip code. 

ection 3 – Restriction Information  

This  section  should  be  used  to  identify  the  restrictions  imposed  by  the  judge.    The  effective  date  and 
termination  dates  should  only  be  indicated  if  the  minor  is  the  holder  of  a  driver’s  license  or  beginner’s 
permit.  When the minor is not licensed, the issuance of a credential will be delayed if the minor makes an 
application before his or her 18th birthday.  
 

•  Boxes 1 – 4 Driving Restrictions 

Use only if the minor is the holder of a beginner’s permit or driver’s license.  In this area the court 
may mark one or all four of the driving restriction boxes.   
 

If  box  4  is  selected,  the  court  must  provide  under  the  area  of  Additional  Instructions  what  the 
driving restrictions are.  

•  Box 4 

•  Box 5 

This  box  should  only  be  selected  when  the  minor  is  not  a  licensed  driver  or  the  holder  of  a 
beginner’s permit.  The DMV will create a customer record for the court ordered restriction and 
will  automatically  impose  the  restriction  if  the  applicant  makes  an  application  for  a  credential 
before his or her 18th birthday.  

•  Additional Instructions 

This area may be used to list any special restrictions the court imposed on the minor (box 4 should 
also be checked).  

ection 4 – Court Information 

This section is used for the court signing off on the driving restrictions imposed.  Provide the date, judge’s 
signature, court name, and court address.  

hen a court later determines that modifications are needed to the Form VS-200, the court is required to
