**In any matter a request to change the mode of proceeding and/or appearance can be requested by motion.   The motion must be filed with a 
supporting affidavit and proposed order. The presiding judge delegates to the juvenile court judges to rule on motions for change of mode of 
proceeding.  Requests to set hearings in DEPENDENCY cases, other than Original Hearings and Shelter Review Hearings, shall be made by motion.  
Requests to set hearings in DELINQUENCY cases shall be made using the DELINQUENCY HEARING REQUEST FORM. The court will review the request 
form the day it is filed and approve or deny the request.  If a requesting party is seeking an expedited decision by the court that designation shall be in 
the caption of the motion or the section for “Type of Hearing Sought and Purpose” on the hearing request form. All requests for hearings shall be e-filed 
on Odyssey.   

**All delinquency cases, other than those specified below, shall be heard in person unless the youth is in a youth correctional facility and/or treatment 
facility that is outside of Lane County.  The court will inquire about, and the parties are expected to address and confirm the mode of proceeding at the 
Initial Appearance on any new delinquency petition.
