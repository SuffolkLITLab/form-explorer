                           EMWENIN OMW KOPWE KUNA IKA PWE OMW WE CASE MI FEN KEPICHINO  
                            (PARDON) 

n November 21, 2022, Governor Kate Brown a amwusano ika pardon ukukun 47,000 aramas mei wor aar 
conviction (tipis/fangeta) ren “possession” ren ew ounce maruwana ika kukun seni, non ekkewe cases ren ewe 
aramas  mi ierini 21 ika nap seni. 

Ese kon ffat ika pwe omw we case mi fen pardon? 

1.  Tungor Poraus seni ewe Oregon Judicial Department: 

En mei tongeni kutta omw case on ewe website Oregon Judicial Department Online Records Search, ese 
kamo. Ika kose kuna omw we case, menip a fen pardon (wewen, ekkewe case ra fen kepichino me sealed, 
rese chiwen nomw non ewe website). Ika ka kuna omw we case, ina epwe pwarata pwe ese pachanong non 
an ewe Governor orderin maruwana pardon, iwe kose chiwen need omw kopwe amasowa ewe “Order 
Request Form” ika Taropwen Tungor Order (pun ese wor order faniten omw we case).  

Omw kutta porausen omw we/kewe case, en mei tongeni watiw itomw (Sainganon, Ewin me Middle) non ewe 
atun omw we conviction, ika ewe nampan case. A tongeni  mecheres omw kut ika ke chok watiw ekkena 
ruwefou mak nepoputan  ewin itomw me nepoputan  omw last  name. Ei mei forata an epwe tam iteiten 
kan case. Katon  ena list pwe kopwe katon ika mei kono itechikin itomw me non na taropwen 
kepwung. 

uttaochu  chok. Menip omw na case e mak fan ew omw kewe nickname ika oruwen  itomw ika itomw 

ke kan iteni  menoomw. Pwan ew, menip efen mwän ranin  uputiwom ika itechikin itomw. 
 

2.  Tungor Poraus seni ewe Oregon State Police 

En mei pwan tongeni tungor echo kapin omw we criminal history seni ewe Oregon State Police, ikei. Kopwe 
amasowa  ewe taropwe, fori omw fingerprints, me monatiw ew $33.00 fee faniten angangan. Ren soposopwun 
poraus faniten forun Criminal History Record Checks, kose mochen kopwe tota won ewe Oregon State Police 
website.  

Kopwe aea ew address ia kopwe chok akkangei taropwe si tiin ngonuk, pwun mei tongeni epwe tori 3 maram 
ach sipwe sökuruk ren ponuen omw tungor. Ika omw we maruwana conviction ese pachanong non omw 
criminal history, menip a fen pardon.   

3.  Tungor Kapin ewe Court Record ren Seal-en Omw Case  

Ika ka nuku pwe omw we case mei pachanong non an ewe Governor orderin pardon iwe ke mochen angei 
echo kapin ewe order, kose mochen amasowa ei Taropwen Tungor Order ika Order Request Form. Ekkewe 
kapi mi free. 

Ewe Judicial Department mei tongeni angang won omw ei tungor non ukukun 90 ran, ika napeseni, epwe 
pwan tinano kapin ewe Order to Seal ngeni ewe address en mei fen watiw non ewe Order Request Form ika 
Taropwen Tungor Order. Ika sise kuna omw we case are/ika sia need pwan ekkoch poraus sonuk, sipwe 
esinesin ngonuk seni mail.  

         

  O

  K

Met kopwe fori ne kut ekkewe case records me online
