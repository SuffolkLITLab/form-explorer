rongful Conviction Compensation 

In 2022, the Oregon Legislative Assembly passed Senate Bill (SB) 1584 (Oregon Laws 2022, 
chapter 105).  The measure allows a wrongfully convicted person to file a petition seeking 
compensation in the Marion County Circuit Court or in the circuit court for the county of conviction, 
and directs OSCA to adjust these compensation amounts annually according to the methodology 
described in the measure.  The proposed adjusted amounts are listed below:
