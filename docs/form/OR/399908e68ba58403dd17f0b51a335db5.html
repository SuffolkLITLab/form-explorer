Legal Glossary 

his English-Burmese Legal Glossary has been developed by the National Center for State Courts (NCSC) 
as a resource and working document for court interpreters. The 398 English terms in this legal glossary 
were selected from the NCSC’s Glossary of Commonly Used Court & Justice System Terminology as 
terms that are more frequently used in state courts. Users of the English-Burmese Legal Glossary may 
refer to this source document for definitions of the selected English terms. Note that the list of selected 
English terms is neither comprehensive nor complete. 

This NCSC has made reasonable attempts to ensure that the translations in this legal glossary are 
accurate; however, the NCSC cannot guarantee their accuracy. This legal glossary is not intended to 
provide legal advice and the NCSC assumes no legal liability or responsibility for the accuracy of the 
translations contained therein. 

Legal terminology may vary depending on alternate meanings as well as the specific context in which 
it is used.
