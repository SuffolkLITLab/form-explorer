et sakkun tipis epwe mwus seni an ewe Governor pardon order? 

On November 21, 2022, Governor Brown amwusano tipisin ekkewe mi wor rer ew (1) ounce ika kukun 
seni ukukun maruwana me non fonuwan Oregon, ren ekkoch cases mi wor online mi tori ekkei unungat 
kinikin:  

●  Ewin, ewe chok charge non ewe case fanitan omw anomwu ew ounce ren maruwana ika kukun

meinisin case mi pachenong nenian me uwouwan ika neni epwe no ngeni.  

●  Aruwan, ewe case ese pachenong ewe victim.  
●  Aunungatin, ewe aramasen ewe case mi ierini 21 non ewe fansoun mi angei maruwana.

AN GOVERNOR KATE BROWN WE MARUWANA PARDON 
 

Met ewe pardon order mi pwan epichano ekkewe charge ese kamotiw mi pwan riri ngeni ewe 
conviction ika violation? 
Ewer. An Governor amwusomwus mi pwan apichano ina epwe ukukun  $14 million ren ekkewe rese 
monatiw ar charges fanitan chok ewe pardon convictions. Fanitan ei, ewe State ese chiwen angei mön, 
nge epwe meni tori fitu wiik ren an state epwe atou an records me tungor ngeni ekkewe collection 
agencies aar repwe weifini ekkewe charges mi pachenong non an Governor we pardon. Ewe State a 
resin uti meinisin records an epwe updated an epwe clearini nusun met mi nomw on ekkewe case non 
ukukun  ew maram ren an Governor we mwokutukut.  

pwe ifa usun ika inet epwe pichino tipisin ai we pwupwungaw fanitan maruwana? 
Ika omw conviction epwe tori ukukun  ei kinikin mi affat asan, non ukukun ew ran ranin an Governor 
Brown we pardon order on November 21, 2022, ewe circuit court me non ewe county ren conviction 
epwe sealini ewe case ren pardoned convictions, ikewe epwe ne fis an epwe amworano ewe case me 
conviction seni omw official court record. Echo kapin ewe court order mi seal omw record on epwe 
katou non ukukun 6-8 wiik. Ika en mi need echo kapin ewe circuit court order fanitan omw we case, iwe 
ka tongeni tungor echo kapi seni ei link: https://www.courts.oregon.gov/forms/Pages/marijuana-
pardon.aspx.   

et epwe fis ngeni ai we criminal record murin an seal me court iwe epwe pwan ifa nikinikin ewe 

pardoned conviction me on ai criminal history?  
Ewe pardoned maruwana conviction esapw chiwen pwaata on omw background check ren public court 
records. Iwe ewe conviction epwe meni pwa on background checks seni law enforcement officials (polis) 
ika licensing authorities, nge epwe pwaata fanitan pardoned conviction. Pwan sopwosopwun, ekkoch 
private companies repwe meni angei ekkei data/porous mi pachangeni convictions me mwan ranin an 
Governor we pardon, epwe seni ewe contract ngeni State ika seni aar angei ekkei data/poraus seni 
public sites me on internet. 

ka omw we conviction mi pardoned, ewe State epwe atoura ngeni ekkewe private companies ren data 
agreements ngeni state courts pun ewe convictions mi pardoned iwe nap ngenir repwe amorano ewe 
pardoned cases seni ar records. Iwe kopwe pwan sinei pun State ese wor an nemenem ren poraus mi 
nomw me katou seni private companies iwe ewe company epwe meni u ngeni ika esapw tongeni 
amorano met mi nomw fanitan ewe pardon conviction. Ika en mi nuku pun omw we case a fen 
kepichino iwe en mi sinei pun ewe private company mi anomwu recording me pwan pwaata on ewe 
background check, kose mochen mak ngeni ei address: OJD.order.request@ojd.state.or.us.   

et repwe pwan asinei ngeniei ika ai we conviction a fen mwusono? 

Apw. Ekkewe porous mi kawor seni Oregon Judicial Department ren ekkei pardons ese pachenong 
ekkoch identifying information (porous tichik) ren addresses ika ekkoch demographic information. Iwe 
ewe offesin Governor ese pwan anomwu an emon me emon contact information me an io conviction a 
pichino iwe ren ei esapw asinei ngeni emon me emon. 

wata upwe sinei ika ai we case ren maruwana conviction mi pachenong non ewe pardon? 
Ika omw we maruwana conviction me case mi churi ewe ukukun mi affat asan, iwe an ewe Governor 
pardon ren omw conviction me an ewe court sealini ren ewe case record epwe sopwosopwono. Ekkewe 
mi wor ar pardoned convictions esapw meni ffat pun aar record a fen sealed.

win, omw maruwana conviction me case rese meni qualified ren ewe pardon anongonong won an 
Governor aukuk. Usun met mi affat me asan, Governor Brown a epichano state-level convictions ren 
possession ren ew ounce maruwana ika kukun seni me non Oregon, ese pachenong municipal me justice 
court convictons. Emon mi convicted epwe ierini 21 feita non ena atun epwe angei maruwana. Met chok 
e charge me non ei case epwe chok fanitan an angei ew (1) ounce ika kukun seni ren maruwana, wewen 
pun esapw pwan wor ekkoch charges epwe fael seni law enforcement me non ena case, iwe ena case 
esapw pwan pachenong forun ika delivery/amomono maruwana. Amwuchunon, ewe case mi 
pachenong pardon conviction non ese apachanong emon victim. Ika omw conviction ika case ese tori ew 
me nein ekkei kinikin iwe omw we maruwana conviction me noomw ese pichino.  

ruwan, ren ekkoch convictions–akasainon ekkewe me mwan 2005–repwe meni esapw naf porausan 
me ren State ika ewe conviction mi tori ewe aukuk Governor mi affata non an we pardon order mi affat 
asan. Ren ffatan, ekkewe convictions ese naf porausan esapw pachenong non ewe kamwus an 
Governor. 

wata an Governor we maruwana pardon e chok apply ngeni State-level convictions?  
Ewe Statewide judicial database mi aea seni Oregon Judicial Department ese wor an access ngeni 
ekkewe city me county municipal ika justice court records. Ika ke mochen sinei ekkoch porousan “set-
aside” ren court records (wewen aturano) faniten ew maruwana conviction me non justice ika municipal 
court, ke tongeni contactini ewe court ia omw we conviction ekan fis me ia. 

et an Governor we kamwus ren maruwana mi apply ngeni convictions me non ekkoch neni ika ewe 

aramas iei e nom Oregon?  
Apw. Governor Brown mi wor an authority/nemenem an epwe pardon convictions me non chok Oregon. 

et an Governor we maruwana pardon mi pwan apply ngeni ekkoch ika re amomo maruwana, mi 

nap seni ew (1) ounce, ika fen wor aar conviction ren maruwana? 
Apw. An Governor Brown we order ren maruwana pardon e chok apply ngeni state-level convictions ren 
possession ren ew (1) ounce ika kukun  seni ren maruwana me non Oregon, ren chok ew case ese wor 
ewe victim ren an ewe aramas mi ierini 21 ika nap seni.  

kkefa ekkena demographics ren emon me emon ren komwusan aar convictions?  
Porausan ekkewe case seni Governor ese mwo pachenong ekkoch demographic information.  

ite ukukun meen takises ei kamwus ika angangen pardon?  
Ewe kamwus mei watte met mi katou seni an state resources. Ese wor moni mi katou seni offesin 
Governor; ewe Oregon Judicial Department a uwau fan ew chok ei moni seni legislature ren court staff 
an epwe amwokutu court orders me awasano seal-en ei mwokutukut.  

we met upwene fori ika ai we maruwana conviction ese mwusetiw, nge ai nuku epwe fen 
kamwusetiw? 
Ika omw we conviction ese mwuseno, iwe kopwe apply ngeni ewe court an epwe angei omw we 
conviction a “set-aside” (wewen aturano). Porausan ena mwokutukut mi pachenong non ekkena kapas 
eis fan. Wewe chok ika kopwe tungor ren kamwusetiwan ew conviction ese pachenong non an Governor 
we order fanitan maruwana pardon, ka tongeni apply ngeni ew pardon omw kopwe uwanong ei 
taropwe ngeni an offesin Governor. Foforun  ewe taropwen pardon mi pachenong case-by-case 
evaluation ren noum we application ika taropwe seni Governor me chon angang non an Governor we

fa usun angangan taropwen ewe traditional pardon, mi pachenong ren maruwana convictions

pwe ifa ne apply ngeni ai upwe aturano ika sealini maruwana conviction ika ese pachenong non an 

Governor we maruwana pardon?  
Non Oregon ese offer “sealing” ika “expungement” (kamorono) ren adult criminal records, nge mi offer 
“set-asides” nupwen ewe record epwe set-aside me non Oregon, ewe court epwe set aside ewe record

orderini ewe record fanitan ewe conviction iwe meinisin ekkewe ekkoch official records, pachenong law 
enforcement records, an epwe sealed. Ikkei pwan ekkoch information epwe anisuk omw kopwe apply
