COURTS’ PUBLIC SERVICE HOURS BY LOCATION 
(As of January 1, 2020) 

he Office of the State Court Administrator (OSCA) has assembled this information for the convenience 

of court users.  This table shows the public service hours for the Oregon Supreme Court, Court of 
Appeals, Tax Court, and circuit courts.  It is based information submitted to OSCA by those courts. 

ARNING.  Court hours are subject to change and may vary from the hours listed in this table due to 
closures for weather, emergencies, and other factors.  If you must appear in court or submit a filing on a 
specific day, you may want to verify the hours of operation for that day with the court.  You can find a 
telephone number for each court in the “Court Location” column of this table.  You can also find contact 
and other court information on our home page: https://www.courts.oregon.gov/Pages/default.aspx.  Go 
to the “Find a Court” box, click on “Select Your Court,” and then click on the appropriate court name.
