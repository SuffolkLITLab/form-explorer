INFORMATION ABOUT CONTINUING EDUCATION COMPLIANCE 

REQUIREMENT: 
All  certified  and  registered  courtroom  interpreters  must  annually  complete  at  least  eight  (8)  hours  of 
continuing education approved by the State Board of Examiners of Certified Courtroom Interpreters which 
shall include at least two (2) hours relating to Oklahoma court rules and procedures or Ethics. 

AUTOMATIC SUSPENSION: 
Failure to earn the required continuing education hours, to submit a completed compliance report, and/or to 
pay any applicable continuing education penalty fee on or before February 15 shall result in administrative 
suspension on that date.  See Title 20, Chap. 23, App. II, Rule 20.
