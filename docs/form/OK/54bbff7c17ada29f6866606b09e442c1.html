Information:    All  persons  enrolled  as  certified  shorthand  reporters  must annually  renew 
their certificate by submitting the attached form along with the renewal fee in the amount of 
Thirty Dollars ($30.00). 

2 ways to pay your renewal fee: 

1. By credit card:  Call (405) 556-9862 OR (405) 556-9673.
2. With check or money order:  Mail check or money order payable to Clerk of the
Supreme Court and include your CSR number on your check or money order.

Failure  to  renew  your  certificate  and  pay  the  annual  renewal  fee  on  or  before  February  15, 
2023 shall result in administrative suspension on that date.  Every certificate, which has not 
been renewed on or before February 15, shall be subject to a delinquent payment fee in the 
amount of $100.00.  If you are submitting this form after February 15, you must include both 
the  renewal  fee  ($30.00)  and  the  delinquent  payment  fee  ($100.00).   A suspended  certificate, 
which has not been reinstated on or before April 15, shall be administratively revoked on that 
date.  See 20 O.S. Ch. 20, App. 1, Rule 20. 

If  you  are  a  certified  shorthand  reporter  employed  by  the  District  Courts  of  Oklahoma  and 
become eligible for an increase in salary through recognition by the National Court Reporters 
Association  (NCRA)  as  a  CP  (or  equivalent),  CM  (or  equivalent),  RMR,  RDR, CRR,  or  RPR, 
written notice must be given to the Administrative Office of the Courts as soon as the results 
are  received  from  the  NCRA  along  with  a  photocopy  of  the  test  results.    Adjustments  to  the 
salary  of  the  reporter  will  be  made  beginning  on  the  first  day  of  the  first  month  following 
receipt of the information by the Administrative Office.
