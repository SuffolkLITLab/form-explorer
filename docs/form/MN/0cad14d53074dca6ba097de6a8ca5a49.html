8.  The adult adoptee is at least 18 years old, is not considered a vulnerable adult under Minn.

10. As written in the consent, the adult adoptee understands that adoption: 

a.  Establishes a legal parent-child relationship between the adopting parent and the 

person adopted, including the right to inherit; and

and their birth parents and siblings according to Minn. Stat. §259.59.  Note: the
