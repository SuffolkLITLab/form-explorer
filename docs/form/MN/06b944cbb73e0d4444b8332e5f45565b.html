hat is a Harassment Restraining Order? 

Ntawv Yuam Ceev Kev Zes Ua Phem (Harassment restraining order) yog dab tsi? 

 Harassment Restraining Order (HRO) is an order signed by a judicial officer (judge or referee) 
that orders someone to stop harassing you and have no contact, unless allowed in the court order. 
It is not a criminal proceeding and takes place in civil court.  
Daim ntawv Yuam Ceev Kev Zes Ua Phem (Harassment restraining order) (HRO) yog ib daim 
ntawv yuam kos npe los ntawm tus kws ceev kev ncaj ncees (tus kws phua txim los sis tus saib 
kev ncaj ncees) uas yuam ib tug neeg kom tsum txoj kev zes ua phem rau koj thiab tsis txhob 
muaj kev sib cuag nrog koj, tshwj tsis yog lub tsev hais plaub kheev nyob rau hauv daim ntawv 
yuam. Nws tsis yog ib txoj kev hais plaub ntug ua neeg txhaum cai thiab yuav muab hais rau 
hauv lub tsev hais pej xeem plaub ntug.
