Important Notices and Resources/Ogeysiisyo iyo Macluumaadyo Muhiim ah 

The Court has forms and instructions, for some types of cases, as a general guide to the court process. 
These instructions explain the steps in more detail and answer common questions, but are not a full 
guide to the law. Court employees may be able to give general information on court rules and 
procedures, but they cannot give legal advice. 
Maxkamaddu waxay haysaa foomam iyo habraacyo, noocyada kiisaska qaarkood, guud ahaan si 
hoggaan u ah habraaca maxkamadda. Habraacyadaas waxa ay si faahfaahsan u sharxayaan oo ka 
jawaabayaan su'aalo guud, laakiin sharci ahaan ma aha hoggaan buuxa. Shaqaalaha maxkamadda 
ayaa laga heli karaa macluumaad guud oo ku saabsan habka iyo habraacyada maxkamadaha, laakiin 
qofna ma siin karaan talo la xiriirta sharciga.

General Information/Macluumaad Guud 

If you cannot afford to pay for an attorney, you may qualify for a court-appointed attorney. Under the 
law, the court may appoint an attorney if: 
Haddii aadan iska bixin karin lacagta qareenka, waxaad xaq u yeelan karaa qareen maxkamaddu kuu 
qabato. Sida sharcigu dhigayo, maxkamaddu waxay qareen kuu qaban kartaa haddii: 

1.  You or any legal dependent living in your household are receiving public assistance under 

one or more of the following means-tested government benefit programs: 
Adiga ama qof aad sharci ahaan masuul ka tahay oo guriga kugula nool waa in aad 
qaadataan kaalmo dowladeed sabatoo ah mid ama in ka badan oo ah barnaamijyo kaalmo 
dowladeed oo la idiin oggolaaday: 

Minnesota Supplemental Assistance (MSA) Programs; Minnesota Family Investment 
Program (MFIP); Food Stamps (EBT Card); General Assistance; MinnesotaCare; Medical 
Assistance; Energy Assistance; and Supplemental Security Income (SSI). 
Barnaamijyada Minnesota Supplemental Assistance (MSA); Minnesota Family Investment 
Program (MFIP); Food Stamps (Kaarka EBT); General Assistance; MinnesotaCare; Medical 
Kaalmo; Kaalmada Korontada; iyo Gunnada Dadka Hawlgabay (SSI).

2.  Your assets and income are not enough to pay for the reasonable costs charged by a private 
attorney in your area while also paying for the common necessities of life for yourself and

and that you could easily sell. Your house and car are not included.

Haddii aad rumeysan tahay in aad ka soo bixi karto mid ka mid ah shuruudaha kore, waxaad soo

Step 1/Tallaabada 1-aad 
Fill Out Affidavit for Court-Appointed Attorney (CHP1002) 
Soo Buuxi Foomka Markhaatikaca Qareenka Maxkamaddu Qabato 
(CHP1002) 

If you need help completing the Affidavit, let a court administration staff person at your local county 
courthouse know. Once the Affidavit is complete, sign the Affidavit under penalty of perjury. This 
means you are stating that the information in the Affidavit is true to the best of your knowledge. 
Haddii aad u baahan tahay in lagaa caawiyo foomka Markhaatikaca, u sheeg shaqaalaha degmada 
maxkamadda aad ku nooshahay. Marka foomka Markhaatikaca  ah la buuxiyo, ku saxiix foomka 
Markhaatikaca ciqaabta dhaarta beenta ulakaca ah. Taas macnaheedu waa in aad sheegeyso in 
macluumaadka ku dhex qoran foomka Markhaatikaca uu yahay run sida kuugu fiican ee aad adigu 
u ogtahay. 
Perjury is the crime of intentionally lying or misrepresenting the truth, punishable by jail or other 
sanctions. 
Dhaarta beenta ah waa danbi ah in si ulakac ah been loo sheegay ama runtii looga leexday, 
ciqaabtiisuna waa xabsi ama cawaaqibxumo kale. 

Step 2/Tallaabada 2-aad 
File Affidavit for Court-Appointed Attorney with Court Administration 
U soo gudbi Foomka Markhaatikaca Qareenka Maxkamaddu Qabato 
Maamulka Maxkamadda 

Provide the completed affidavit to the Court Administrator so it can be filed in your case. The court 
administration staff person will tell you the procedures for having a judicial officer review your 
application. 
U keen foomka markhaatikaca oo buuxsan Maamulka Maxkamadda si loogu xareeyo kiiskaaga. 
Shaqaalaha maxkamadda ayaa kuu sheegaya habraaca aad ku heleyso in uu garsoore go'aaminaya 
kuu eego codsigaaga. 

What to Expect Next…/Waxaad Filaneyso ee Kuugu Xiga... 

If the judicial officer grants your request for a court appointed attorney, the order will only apply 
to the court case listed in the caption of the order. Court Administration will assign you to a 
specific court-appointed attorney and notify you and the attorney of the assignment. The court- 
appointed attorney will represent your interests in the case until court jurisdiction is terminated
