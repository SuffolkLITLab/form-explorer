Definitions/Lus Tshab Txhais: 

Race Categories/Cov Pawg Haiv Noob Neeg: * 

American Indian or Alaska Native: A person having origins in any of the original peoples of North and South 
America (including Central America), and who maintains tribal affiliation or community attachment. 
Neeg Khab Asmesliskas los sis Khab Aslasxaskas: Ib tug neeg chiv keeb tawm ntawm haiv noob neeg uas ib 
txwm nyob rau Asmesliskas Qaum Teb thiab Qab Teb (koom nrog rau Asmesliskas Nrab Teb), thiab tseem 
muaj feem cuam koom nrog cov pawg neeg ntawd. 

Asian: A person having origins in any of the original peoples of the Far East, Southeast Asia, or the Indian 
subcontinent including, for example, Cambodia, China, India, Hmong, Japan, Korea, Malaysia, Pakistan, the 
Philippine Islands, Thailand, and Vietnam.  
Neeg Esxias: Ib tug neeg uas chiv keeb tawm ntawm cov haiv neeg uas ib txwm nyob rau Esxias Sab Hnub 
Tuaj, Esxias Qab Teb Sab Hnub Tuaj, los is lub teb chaws Eesdias koom nrog rau, xws li teb chaws Khabmeem, 
Suav, Eesdias, Hmoob, Nyivpooj, Kauslim, Maslesxias, Pov Txwv Fislispeej, Thaib Teb, thiab Nyablaj Teb. 

Black or African American: A person having origins in any of the black racial groups of Africa, for example 
Somalia. Terms such as “Haitian” can be used in addition to “Black or African American.” 
Dub los sis Esfasliskees Asmesliskas: Ib tug neeg uas chiv keeb ntawm cov haiv neeg dub uas ib txwm nyob 
rau teb chaws Esfasliskas, xws lis Xosmoslias. Cov lus tias “Haitian” siv tau ntxiv rau cov lus tias “Neeg Dub los 
sis Esfasliskees Asmeslikas” thiab. 

Native Hawaiian or Other Pacific Islander: A person having origins in any of the original peoples of Hawaii, 
Guam, Samoa, or other Pacific Islands. 
Neeg Hasvais los sis cov neeg nyob Pov txwv Pasxisfis: Ib tug neeg uas chiv keeb tawm ntawm cov haiv neeg 
uas ib txwm nyob teb chaws Hasvais, Nguas, Xasmuas, los sis lwm cov pov txwm Pasxisfiv. 

White: A person having origins in any of the original peoples of Europe, the Middle East, North Africa, or Mexico. 
Neeg Dawb: Ib tug neeg uas chiv keeb tawm ntawm cov haiv neeg uas ib txwm nyob teb chaws Yuslauv, sab 
Mivdaum Iv, Esfasliskas Qaum Teb, los sis Mevxiskaum. 

Ethnicity/Keeb Haiv Neeg: * 

Hispanic or Latino: A person of Cuban, Mexican, Puerto Rican, South or Central American, or other Spanish 
culture or origin, regardless of race. The term, “Spanish origin,” can be used in addition to “Hispanic or Latino.” 
Neeg Hisxaspasniv los sis Lastisnaus: Ib tug neeg uas chiv keeb tawm ntawm teb chaws Cuba, Mexico, Puerto 
Rico, Asmesliskas Qab Teb log sis Nrab Teb, los sis cov neeg teb chaws Spanish, tsis hais tias yuav yog haiv neeg 
dab tsi kuj xij. Los lus tias, “Noob Spanish,” kuj siv tau dua li ntawm ob lo lus “Hispanic los sis Latino” thiab 

* The United States Census Bureau has established these Race and Ethnicity categories. 
* Teb Chaws Asmesliskas lub Tuam Tsev Suav Pej Xeem tau tsim tawm cov pawg Haiv Noob Neeg thiab Keeb 
Haiv Neeg no.
