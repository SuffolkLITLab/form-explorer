By signing this Affidavit, I am certifying that these statements are true under penalty of 
perjury. I understand that if I provide false information on the form it may lead to 
criminal charges. I understand that failure to execute the form or failure to provide 
information or requested records may result in denial of my request. I am authorizing that 
the facts contained in this Affidavit may be verified by the court. I understand that if I am 
appointed an attorney, I have a continuing duty to notify the court of any changes to my 
financial circumstances.

oo la igu qaadi karo ciqaabta dhaarta beenta ulakaca ah. Waxaan fahamsanahay in haddii
