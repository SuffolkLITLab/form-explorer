EPC Hearing Contact List 

INSTRUCTIONS:  Pursuant to Rule 42.04 of the Rules of Juvenile Protection Procedure, the 
court administrator or designee (such as social services) is required to file in the court file a list 
of parties, participants, and attorneys notified and attempted to be notified of the date, time, and 
location  of  the  EPC  Hearing.    At  the  hearing,  the  list  will  assist  the  judge  to  determine  what 
further  efforts  must  be  made  to  contact  any  absent  individuals  to  inform  them  of  any  future 
hearings.    Consistent  with  the  “Party/Participant  Status”  list,  this  EPC  Hearing  Contact  list 
identifies potential parties, participants, and attorneys so that each may be timely notified of the 
EPC Hearing.
