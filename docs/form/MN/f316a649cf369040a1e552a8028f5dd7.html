Under the Minnesota Domestic Violence Act, an Order for 
Protection must be personally served on the respondent within 
14 days from the date of the order. If there is no hearing 
scheduled in your case, it is your responsibility to know 
whether the sheriff’s department has been able to serve the 
order within 14 days. You or your advocate can contact the 
sheriff’s department in the county where the order is being served 
to check on the status.
