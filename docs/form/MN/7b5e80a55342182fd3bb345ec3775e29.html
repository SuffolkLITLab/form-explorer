Definitions/Qeexid: 

 

Race Categories/Noocyada Isika: * 

merican Indian or Alaska Native: A person having origins in any of the original peoples of North and South 

America (including Central America), and who maintains tribal affiliation or community attachment. 
Hindida Mareykanka ama Dhaladka Alaska: Qof asal ahaan ka soo jeeda mid ka mid ah dadka Waqooyiga iyo 
Koonfurta Qaaradda Mareykanka (oo ay ku jiraan Bartamaha Ameerika), oo qofkaasi waxa uu ka tirsan yahay 
qabiil ama bulsho uu ku xiran yahay. 

Asian: A person having origins in any of the original peoples of the Far East, Southeast Asia, or the Indian 
subcontinent including, for example, Cambodia, China, India, Hmong, Japan, Korea, Malaysia, Pakistan, 
the Philippine Islands, Thailand, and Vietnam. 
Aasiyaan: Qof asal ahaan ka soo jeeda mid ka mid ah dadka ku nool Bariga Qaaradda Aasiya, Koonfurbari 
Aasiya, ama Dalalka Koonfurta Aasiya, tusaale ahaan, Kaamboodiya, Shiinaha, Hindiya, Moong, Jabbaan, 
Kuuriya, Maleeyshiya, Baakistaan, Filimbiin, Taaylaan, iyo Fiyetnaam. 

Black or African American: A person having origins in any of the black racial groups of Africa, for example 
Somalia. Terms such as “Haitian” can be used in addition to “Black or African American.” 
Madow ama Madowga Mareykanka: Qof asal ahaan ka soo jeeda mid ka mid ah dalalka Afrika, tusaale 
ahaan Soomaaliya. Dadka kale ee madow sida “Hayiitiyaanka” waxa ay ka mid yihiin “Madow ama Madowga 
Mareykanka.” 

Native Hawaiian or Other Pacific Islander: A person having origins in any of the original peoples of Hawaii, 
Guam, Samoa, or other Pacific Islands. 
Dhaladka Haawaay ama Jasiiradaha Kale ee Baasifigga: Qof asal ahaan ka soo jeeda mid ka mid ah dadka 
Haawaay, Guwaam, Samoowa, ama Jasiiradaha kale ee Baasifigga. 

White: A person having origins in any of the original peoples of Europe, the Middle East, North Africa, or Mexico. 
Caddaan: Qof asal ahaan ka soo jeeda mid ka mid ah dadka Yurub, Bariga Dhexe, Waqooyiga Afrika, ama Meksiko. 

Ethnicity/Qowmiyad: * 

Hispanic or Latino: A person of Cuban, Mexican, Puerto Rican, South or Central American, or other Spanish 
culture or origin, regardless of race. The term, “Spanish origin,” can be used in addition to “Hispanic or Latino.” 
Xisbaanig ama Laatiino: Isir kasta oo uu ka soo jeedaba, qof dhaqan ahaan inta badan la wadaaga dalalka ku 
hadla afka Isbaanishka, sida Kuubba, Meksiko, Boorto Riiko, Koonfurta ama Bartamaha Qaaradda Ameerika. 
Marka la yiraahdo, “asal ahaan Isbaanish,” waxaa loola jeedaa “Xisbaanig ama Laatiino.” 

 The United States Census Bureau has established these Race and Ethnicity categories. 
* Xafiiska Tirakoobka Mareykanka ayaa go’aamiyey noocyada Isirrada iyo Qowmiyadaha.
