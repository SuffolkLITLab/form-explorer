ask. Under Minn. R. Pub. Access 4, subd. 1(a)(2), if an address or phone is confidential, the

disclosed only to court staff or law enforcement for purposes of service of process, conducting 
an investigation, or enforcing an order. Your request only applies to this Harassment 
Restraining Order (HRO) case. There may be other documents already filed in this case, or
