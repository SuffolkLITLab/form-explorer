XPUNGEMENT OR SEALING OF A RECORD – WHAT IT MEANS 

KEV TSHEM TAWM LOS YOG KAW NPOG NTAUB NTAWV – NWS TXHAIS LI CAS 
Minn.  Stat.  §  609A  states  that  the  expungement  of  criminal  records  is  limited  to  a  court  order 
sealing records and prohibiting disclosure of their existence or their opening except under court 
order or statutory authority.  Nothing in the law authorizes destruction of records or their return 
to you. A judge of the District Court must decide this petition.
