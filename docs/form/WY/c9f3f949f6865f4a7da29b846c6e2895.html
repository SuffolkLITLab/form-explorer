means  a  person  eighteen  (18)  years  of  age,  however,  for  purposes  of 
child support obligations, a parent's legal obligation for the support of 
his or her children, whether natural or adopted, continues past the age

of  majority  and  twenty  (20)  years  and  attending  high  school  or  an 
equivalent program as full-time participants.)

mandatory  pension  deductions.  Payments  towards  child  support
