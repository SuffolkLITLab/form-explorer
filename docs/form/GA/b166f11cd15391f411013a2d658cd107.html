This form is to be used when filing a petition for leave to sell property which is 
perishable,  liable  to  deteriorate  from  keeping,  or  expensive  to  keep,  pursuant  to 
O.C.G.A. ยง 53-8-11. 

Under  O.C.G.A.  ยง  53-8-11,  the  order  for  sale  may  be  granted  only  after  such 
notice and opportunity for hearing, if any,  as the  Court  deems  practicable  under 
the  circumstances.    Further,  the  sale  shall  be  as  soon  as  practicable  and  in  such 
manner as the Court determines to be in the best interest of the estate.  Therefore, 
all  notice  provisions  set  forth  in  this  form  may  be  altered  by  the  Court  if 
necessary.

  The  Court  finds  that  said  property  is

THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that due to the nature of the property, no 

notice is required prior to the sale.

set forth in the petition.  The petitioner(s) shall file a report of sale, within ten days after the sale, 
reporting the results of the sale.

on the sui juris (heirs of decedent)(beneficiaries who would have received such property under 
the will of decedent).
