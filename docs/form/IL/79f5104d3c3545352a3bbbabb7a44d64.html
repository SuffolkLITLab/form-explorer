The Conference of Chief Circuit Judges, at its meeting on September  I 6, 2022, approved changes 
to the Civil Law Citation and Complaint Form.  The approved  updates are  part of the procedural 
changes  resulting from  the  Public  Act  IO 1-0593  which  authorized  recreational  use of cannabis 
effective January  I, 2020.  Section 3.5 of the Drug Paraphernalia Control Act was removed from 
the  citation  which  originally  contained  violations  for  possession  of drug  paraphernalia.  The 
Printing  Instructions  have  also  been  updated  to  allow Circuit Court  QR  codes,  violator's  email 
address and mobile number and are included with this filing. 

On behalf of the Conference of Chief Circuit Judges,  I hereby  submit the approved citation form 
and  printing instructions to your office for  filing pursuant to Supreme Court  Rule  552.  The new 
form and printing instructions are effective January  I, 2023. 

Agencies  wishing  to  deplete  their current  inventory  may  do  so,  however,  any  new  Civil  Law 
Citations purchased after this update must conform to the form provided herein.
