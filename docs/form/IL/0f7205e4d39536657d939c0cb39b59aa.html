Re: Electronic Overweight Citation and Complaint Form 

The  Conference  of Chief Circuit Judges,  at  its  meeting  on  April  I 2,  2019,  approved  changes  to  the 
Electronic  Overweight  Citation  and  Complaint  Packet  (e-Overweight  Citation  Form  Packet).  The 
approved updates are part of the comprehensive systematic and procedural changes resulting from  the 
enactment of the Criminal and Traffic Assessment Act and  recent updates coordinating the A1ticle  V 
Supreme Court Rules with the Act. 

On  behalf of the  Conference  of Chief Circuit  Judges,  I hereby  submit  the  approved  e-Overweight 
Citation Form  packet to  your office for  filing  pursuant to  Supreme Court Rule 552.  The revised e 
Citation Form packet is effective July 1, 2019.  Electronically issued citations should reflect the correct 
information, provided herein,  on all  citations issued on or after July 1, 2019. 

While anyone may request a copy of the form packet, implementation of an Electronic Overweight 
Citation program is authorized in any county which has previously received approval  to  conduct 
an  electronic citation program by the Conference of Chief Circuit Judges or by the  circuit clerk. 
Law  enforcement  agencies  which  have  not  been  approved  to  conduct  an  electronic  citation 
program must continue to  use the printed Overweight Citation and Complaint Form.

PRINTING and DISPLAY INSTRUCTIONS 

The  attached  electronic  Overweight  citation,  hereafter  referred  to  as  the  "e-Overweight 
Citation,"  document  is  a  copy  which  does  not  purport to  reflect  either spacing  or size  of 
type and therefore the following  is  provided.  Use of thee-Overweight Citation is  limited 
to  truck  overweight  and  permit  violations  under  sections  625  ILCS  5/3-401 ( d),  15-111, 
15-113.1,  15-113.2 or  15-113.3  of the Illinois Vehicle Code. 

(1)  DIMENSIONS 

The overall  dimension of the  uniform  e-Overweight  Citation form  may  conform 
to  the  paper  citations  filed  with  the  Clerk  of the  Supreme  Court,  which  may  be 
printed  on  8  ½ x  11  paper,  or  printed  as  a  continuous  form  a  minimum  of 4  ½ 
inches in width. 

If 8  ½  x  11  paper  is  used,  a  two-column  format  is  required.  The  citation  shall 
appear  on  approximately  the  left  5  inches  of the  page.  The  instructions  to  the 
violator shall appear on the right side of the page. 

If a  continuous  form  is  used,  the  citation  shall  appear  first,  followed  by  the 
instructions  to  the  violator  or  the  instructions  may  appear  on  the  reverse  side  of 
the citation.  This format shall be used for each copy of the e-Overweight Citation 
required to be printed as provided in these Printing Instructions. 

(2)  DEFINITION{S) 

The  definition  of  "paper"  for  an  e-Overweight  Citation  must  comply  with 
Supreme Court Rule and Illinois law and have a minimum of 20-year life quality. 

(3)  NUMBER OF COPIES TO BE PRINTED 

The  following  information  shall  be  printed  on  the  three  (3)  copies  of  the  e 
Overweight Citation. 

(a)  Complaint  Copy  - Citation  Face,  Instructions  to  the  Violator,  Failure  to 
Appear-Admonishments  to  the  Violator,  and  Notice  of Consent  for  Entry 
of Judgment. 

(b)  Violator  Copy  - Citation  Face,  Instructions  to  the  Violator,  Failure  to 
Appear-Admonishments  to  the  Violator,  and Notice  of Consent  for  Entry 
of Judgment. 
Court Communication Copy - Citation Reference, A void Multiple Court 
Appearances, and Guilty Plea and Waiver. 

The  Violator  and  Court  Communication copies  of the  citation  shall  be  printed  at 
the  time  of issuance.  Additional  copies  of the  citation  may  be  printed  at  the 
request  of the  violator,  as  required  by  the  clerk  for  disposition  reporting,  or  as 
may be desired by the issuing agency.
