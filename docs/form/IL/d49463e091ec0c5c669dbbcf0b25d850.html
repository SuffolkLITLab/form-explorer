Petition for Order of Protection 
 
 
 
 
IMPORTANT:  This getting started guide and the instructions are not legal advice. They are only meant to 
 
help you learn how to file for an Order of Protection. Your use of the forms does not guarantee you will be 
 
successful in court. 
 
 
To learn how to fill out the forms and file them with the court, read the HOW TO ASK FOR AN ORDER OF 
PROTECTION instruction sheet and the instructions on the forms. 
 
 
 
If you want to speak with an advocate who may help you, call the Illinois Statewide Domestic Violence 
Hotline at 877-863-6338.
