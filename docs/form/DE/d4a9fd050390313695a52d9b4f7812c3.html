New Applications for a License to Carry a Concealed Deadly Weapon

Instructions 

In order for your new application for a license to carry a concealed deadly weapon to be processed, the following 
steps must be completed: 

1. Photographs

Include two (2) color passport-style photographs of the applicant.
Photograph must be taken within the six-month period immediately preceding the filing of the
application.

2.

Include the statutory filing fee of $65.00.


Cash, Money Order or Personal Check, made payable to “PROTHONOTARY” will be accepted.

3. You must request a letter from the Chief of your agency verifying that you are in good standing with the law

enforcement agency from which you retired.  (see attached)

4. A Psychiatric Waiver must be filed with your application.  (see attached)

5. New Applications

Applications may be filed at any time, but must be filed in the County you reside.
You must file the new application and a duplicate copy.
You may hand deliver your application to the Attorney General’s Office in New Castle County and the
Prothonotary’s Office in Kent and Sussex County, or
If all the requirements are met, mail your application as follows:
