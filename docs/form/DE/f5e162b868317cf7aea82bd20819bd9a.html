IN THE COURT OF CHANCERY 

OF THE STATE OF DELAWARE 

SPECIAL  PROCESS  SERVER REQUIREMENTS 

Beginning May 1, 2008, it will be necessary for all persons serving process for 

Court of Chancery matters to be registered with the Court. This will be a license 
renewable annually by May I. All companies and law firms who have in their employ 
individuals they want to register to be eligible to serve process must pay an initial 
registration fee of $300 and an annual renewal fee of $300.   

n addition, the company/firm must list the names of those individuals within 

their organization that they want to register as process servers. There will be an 
additional fee of $50 per person named, renewable annually as well. Fees paid for the 
company/firm and individuals can be combined on one check made payable to Register 
in Chancery. Checks should be remitted to the attention of Ramona Monsen, Deputy 
Court Administrator, Register in Chancery Wilmington. 

his process will eliminate the need to file Motions and Proposed Orders for 

Special Process Server and beginning May 1, 2008 such Motions and Proposed Orders 
will no longer be accepted. Copies of the approved Application for Designation as a 
Special Process Server will be the document the process server need carry with him to 
show authorization by the Court to serve process. The only thing the Register's Office 
will still require in each individual case is a letter of instructional information to assist 
them in preparing summonses in instances where you still want the Register in Chancery 
to prepare the summonses. Filing of a Praecipe and using the Sheriff is also still an 
option. 

Any questions regarding registration and implementation should be directed to 

Ramona Monsen at (302) 255-0311 or Sheila Boyer (302) 255-0552.
