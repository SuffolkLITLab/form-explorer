attached Certificate of Service completely and sign it. 

Submit the original motion to the Prothonotary in the county where the defendant 
was sentenced. 

If  the  defendant  was  convicted  in  a  court  other  than  Superior  Court  in  the  State  of 
Delaware, it will be necessary to file a civil petition for relief.
