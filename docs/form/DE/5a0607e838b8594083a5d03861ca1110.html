AFFIDAVIT BY INDIVIDUAL OWNER POSTING CASH SECURITY 
 
Cash Bond 
 
This is a cash bond of $________________________, secured by $____________________, in cash deposited 
with the Court. 
 

Forfeiture or Release of the Cash Bond 
 
Forfeiture of the Cash Bond.  This bond may be forfeited if the defendant does not comply with a court’s order.  
The court may immediately order the amount of the bond surrendered to the State of Delaware, including the 
security for the bond, if the defendant does not comply with the conditions of the bond.  The Court may order a 
judgment of forfeiture against the defendant and each surety for the entire amount of the bond, including 
interest and costs. 
Release of the Bond.  The Court may order this bond ended at any time.  This bond will be satisfied and the 
security will be released when either:  (1)  the defendant is found not guilty on all charges, (2)  the defendant 
reports to serve a sentence, or (3) the Court has modified the bail.
