4) If this Writ is directed to a financial institution, federal law requires the institution to protect Social Security, 
SSI, SSDI, Veterans’ Benefits, federal retirement system, and certain other exempt funds in the amount of 
exempt funds deposited into the account in the two months prior to the service of this writ of attachment, even if 
commingled with non-exempt funds.  See 31 C.F.R. part 212.
