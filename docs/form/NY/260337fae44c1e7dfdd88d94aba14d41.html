Instructions  for  a  Custody / Visitation  Petition

NCFC  4/2015

Do not make copies of these instructions, as they are for your information only.

All forms must be completed with black ink only, and printed legibly.

To satisfy the legal requirements to have your petition for custody/visitation considered, every
question must be answered on the petition and applicable forms, and required documents must be
attached.  Failure to do so may result in the dismissal of your petition.

These forms and instructions are available in the General Clerk’s Office, Room 108, in the Nassau County
Family Court at 1200 Old Country Road, Westbury, NY and on the Nassau County Family Court website at
www.nycourts.gov/courts/10jd/nassau/family.shtml .

of copies).  Staple each set.  Bring the 4 complete sets of papers to the General Clerk’s Office, Room
108 of the Family Court between the hours of 9:00 a.m. and 4:45 p.m.
