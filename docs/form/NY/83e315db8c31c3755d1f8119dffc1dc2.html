PARTIES WHO, IF THEY OBJECT TO THE COST OF LIVING ADJUSTMENT, SHALL HAVE THE
RIGHT TO BE HEARD BY THE COURT AND TO PRESENT EVIDENCE WHICH THE COURT WILL
CONSIDER IN ADJUSTING THE CHILD SUPPORT ORDER IN ACCORDANCE WITH SECTION FOUR
HUNDRED  THIRTEEN  OF  THE  FAMILY  COURT  ACT,  KNOWN  AS  THE  CHILD  SUPPORT
STANDARDS ACT.1

(2) A PARTY SEEKING SUPPORT FOR ANY CHILD(REN) RECEIVING FAMILY ASSISTANCE SHALL

WITHOUT FURTHER APPLICATION BY ANY PARTY.  ALL PARTIES WILL RECEIVE A COPY OF
THE ADJUSTED ORDER.

WHICH WAS REVIEWED AND ADJUSTED OCCURRING ON OR AFTER THE EFFECTIVE DATE OF
THE ADJUSTED ORDER, REGARDLESS OF WHETHER OR NOT THE PARTY HAS RECEIVED A
COPY OF THE ADJUSTED ORDER.
