Probate Court Form No. 117
(Schedule A)
Receipts on Principal

SCHEDULE A

RECEIPTS ON PRINCIPAL

Assets which are listed on the inventory should be given the same valuation here.  Assets acquired
subsequent to the date of death should be valued as of the date acquired.  Show initial or carrying value and
current fair market value.  Assets should be listed in the following categories: real estate; stocks and bonds;
cash; and tangible personal property.

Probate Court Form No. 117
(Schedule B)
Gains and Losses on Sales
and Other Dispositions

SCHEDULE B

GAINS AND LOSSES ON SALES AND OTHER DISPOSITIONS

State the date and nature of each transaction, showing net proceeds and gain or loss.  Show total gains

and losses and net gain or loss for account period.

Probate Court Form No. 117
(Schedule C)
Disbursements on Principal

SCHEDULE C

DISBURSEMENTS ON PRINCIPAL

Each disbursement should be itemized showing date, amount, recipient and reason for disbursement.
Disbursements must be grouped in the following categories: federal and state taxes; fees; administration
expenses; and general disbursements.  The subtotal for each category should be stated.  Any attorney fees
must be justified as provided in Rule 66 (c) of the Vermont Rules of Probate Procedure.  Trustee’s fees must
also be justified.

SCHEDULE D

DISTRIBUTION OF PRINCIPAL TO BENEFICIARIES

State  for  each  distribution:  date,  nature  of  distribution,  value  of  distribution,  and  recipient  of
distribution  and  provision  of  trust  if  any  under  which  distribution  is  made.    Show  subtotal  for  each
beneficiary.

PRINCIPLE BALANCE ON HAND

Show each asset with current and acquisition value.  Total the current and acquisition values of all

assets on hand.

Probate Court Form No. 117
(Schedule G)
Receipts of Income

SCHEDULE G

RECEIPTS OF INCOME

Receipts of income should be shown in two categories-dividends and interest with a subtotal for each
category.  Each entry should show the asset on which the income was earned, the time period for which the
income was received and the amount received.  There should be a subtotal for each asset.

Probate Court Form No. 117
(Schedule H)
Disbursements of Income)

SCHEDULE H

DISBURSEMENTS OF INCOME

Show for each disbursement: date, nature of disbursement, recipient and amount.  Disbursements
must be grouped in the following categories: federal and state taxes; fees; administration expenses; and
general disbursements.  The subtotal for each category should be stated.  Attorney’s fees must be justified
as provided in Rule 66(c) of the Vermont Rules of Probate Procedure.
