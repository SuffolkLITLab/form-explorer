CONSENT OF SURVIVING SPOUSE, IF ANY, AND CONSENT OF ALL HEIRS AT LAW 
Please note that 14 V.S.A. Section 107(a) provides that if Consents are filed with the Court from all the heirs 
at law and the surviving spouse, if any, then a Will and/or Codicil may be allowed without a hearing. If all 
consents are not obtained, then the Court shall schedule a hearing and notice shall be given as provided by 
the Rules of Probate Procedure. 

Please see the Instructions for Notifying Interested Persons of a Hearing on the Allowance of a Decedent’s Last Will 
& Testament available at vermontjudiciary.org for further details on who needs to sign the consent form.
