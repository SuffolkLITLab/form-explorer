E-Services Request Form

This form is intended for non-attorney users seeking to register for online electronic case access through the Vermont 
Judiciary’s Public Portal website. This form must be filed with the court, into the case listed below, prior to any services 
being granted. 

Name:  

Today’s Date: 

I am a party in case #: 
If you are a party in multiple cases, you only need to list one. This form will be filed into the case listed. 
Reference this same case number when prompted during the elevated access request process on the Public Portal. 
*Note: elevated remote access to non-attorney case parties is NOT available in Juvenile cases.

My role in that case is: 

Plaintiff 

Defendant 

Petitioner 

Respondent 

Other _ 

My email address for E-Services is  ___________________________________  

 I request online access to my case(s) through the Vermont Judiciary Public Portal. 

Must check each box: 

I understand that this requires additional steps to register and request access on the site at 
https://publicportal.courts.vt.gov/Portal/. 

I understand that the email address listed above must be the one used for Public Portal registration, and 
that  if  I  change  my  email  address  and/or  seek  to  register  using  a  different  email  address  I  must  re-
register and submit new documentation to the court 

I understand that I am responsible for the use of my account including any misuse resulting in improper 
disclosure of non-public information. 

Note: Registration for the services above does not grant access to eFiling, which is a separate registration process and 
which is optional for most non-attorneys. See www.vermontjudicary.org for more details. 

Date 

Signature
