ORDER OF COURT 

AND NOW, this 20th day of November 2020, this Court recognizes, that 

due to the rising number of positive COVID tests in Allegheny County, further 

efforts must be made reduce the amount of people present in court facilities 

while  keeping  the  courts  open  to  the  public.      Accordingly,  the  Court 

temporarily amends its Emergency Operations Order entered on August 31, 

2020  and  it  is  hereby  ORDERED  that  the  following  measures  be  taken 

pursuant to Pa.R.J.A. No. 1952(B)(2) effective Tuesday November 24, 2020 

through Friday December 4, 2020: 

Court of Common Pleas 

During  this  period  of  time,  all  matters,  shall  be  conducted  remotely  via 

Advanced  Communication  Technology  and  no 

in-person  hearings  or 

proceedings shall occur in any division of the Court of Common Pleas other 

than as set forth below. All proceedings that can be conducted remotely shall 

proceed as scheduled. Persons summoned to appear for jury selection from

Tuesday, November 24, 2020 through Friday, December 4, 2020 shall not be 

required to appear.   

Postponements 

•  All proceedings scheduled between November 24, 2020 and December 

4,  2020  that  cannot  be  conducted  using  Advanced  Communication 

Technology shall be postponed to a date certain after December 4, 2020 

pursuant to the procedures for each division. 

•  Parties  with Civil  Division  jury trials  scheduled  within  this  time 

period should contact Civil Division Calendar Control to reschedule the 

jury  trial.  The  Calendar  Control  Department  can  be  contacted  by 

emailing  Denise.seibel@alleghenycourts.us  or,  by  calling  412-350-

5417. 

•  Parties  with  an  in-person  arbitration  hearing  scheduled  during  this 

period may request that their hearing be rescheduled to another date to 

proceed remotely using Advanced Communication Technology.  Parties 

requesting  a  remote  ACT  arbitration  hearing  shall  do  so  utilizing  the 

forms,  instructions  and  protocols  found  on  the  Court’s  website 

at https://www.alleghenycourts.us/civil/arbitration.aspx. 

In-Person Hearings 

  ICC Hearings for incarcerated individuals shall proceed as scheduled in 

the Family Division, with as many participants appearing by Advanced 

Communication  Technology  as  possible,  including  witnesses  and 

attorneys. 

All Court of Common Pleas facilities shall remain open to the public and open 

for case filings including, but not limited to:

Magisterial District Courts 

The operations in the Magisterial District Courts are temporarily modified as 

follows: 

Postponements

•  All Civil and Landlord-Tenant proceedings scheduled between November 

24, 2020 and December 4, 2020 shall be postponed to a date certain 

after December 4, 2020. 

•  Preliminary  hearings  for  individuals  on  bail  scheduled  between 

November  24,  2020  and  December  4,  2020,  shall  be  postponed  to  a

Pittsburgh Municipal Court 

The  operations  in  Pittsburgh  Municipal  Court  are  temporarily  modified  as 

follows: 

Postponements 

  Non-Traffic summary proceedings scheduled between November 24,

date certain after January 1, 2021.
