Human trafficking is a type of

from the exploitation of others – mainly 
through the use of force, fraud or coercion 
to manipulate victims into engaging in sex 
acts or labor/services in exchange for 
something of value. Those under age 18 
who exchange sex for something of value 
are human trafficking victims, regardless of 
whether force, fraud or coercion is involved.
