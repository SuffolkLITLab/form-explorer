NOTICE OF 60-DAY REVIEW HEARING

G.S. 50B-3(a2), (a3)

NOTICE TO THE DEFENDANT: You were ordered to attend an abuser treatment program pursuant to G.S. 50B-3(a)(12). Pursuant to 
G.S. 50B-3(a2), you shall begin regular attendance of the program within 60 days of the entry of the order. You are hereby notified that a 
60-day review hearing will be held on the date, time, and at the location set out below. You should appear at that time for a review of your 
compliance with the part of the order ordering you to attend an abuser treatment program.

NOTICE TO THE PLAINTIFF: The defendant was ordered to attend an abuser treatment program pursuant to G.S. 50B-3(a)(12).  
You are hereby notified that a 60-day review hearing will be held on the date, time, and at the location set out below. You may, but are not 
required to, appear at that time for a review of the defendant’s compliance with the part of the order ordering the defendant to attend an 
abuser treatment program.
