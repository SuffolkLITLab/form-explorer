NOTE:  If a defendant charged with an implied consent offense is unable to make bond, the magistrate must (1) inform the defendant in writing of the  
established procedure to have others appear at the jail to observe the defendant’s condition or administer an additional chemical analysis and 
 
(2) require the defendant to list all persons the defendant wishes to contact and their telephone numbers. A copy of this form must be placed in 
 
 
the case file. G.S. 20-38.4(a)(4).
