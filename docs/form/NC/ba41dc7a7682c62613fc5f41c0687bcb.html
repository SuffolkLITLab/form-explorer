NOTE: Use this form for a criminal or infraction proceeding being conducted by audio and video transmission where the defendant’s right to presence or 
right to confront witnesses is implicated. See G.S. 7A-49.6(e) (“Except as otherwise permitted by law, when the right to confront witnesses or be present is 
implicated in criminal . . . proceedings, the court may not proceed by audio and video transmission unless the court has obtained a knowing, intelligent, and 
voluntary waiver of the defendant’s . . . rights.”); see also G.S. 7A-49.6(i) (“This section is not intended to limit the court’s authority to receive remote testimony 
pursuant to statutes that otherwise permit it, including G.S. 15A-1225.1, 15A-1225.2, 15A-1225.3, 20-139.1, 8C-1, Rule 616, 50A-111, and 52C-3-315(f).”).

NOTE TO CUSTODIAN/COUNSEL: Complete all fields above for the case(s) for which this remote audio-video proceeding is being conducted before the 
defendant’s execution of the waiver below. After the court has addressed the defendant and obtained the defendant’s waiver verbally, and the defendant 
has executed the waiver, sign the waiver as a witness below. Upon completion of the proceeding, deliver this waiver along with any other documents 
resulting from the audio-video proceeding to the clerk’s office for execution by the presiding official and placement in the case record.

As the undersigned defendant in this action, I freely and voluntarily declare that: (check all that apply)

 1. Right to presence (select if the court advised you of your right to be present) 

ACKNOWLEDGMENT OF RIGHTS AND WAIVER

• I have been advised of my right to be present physically for the proceeding described above.
•  I have been advised that I am not required to waive my right to be present, and if I do not waive that right, this proceeding will be 

conducted in person and will not be unreasonably delayed as a result.

•  If represented by an attorney, I have had the opportunity to discuss with my attorney my right to appear in person and understand 

that I will be able to speak with my attorney privately during this proceeding.

•  I fully understand my right to appear in person and appreciate the consequences of my decision to waive this right.
•  I therefore freely, voluntarily, and knowingly waive my right to be present physically for this proceeding, and I consent to 

conducting this proceeding by audio and video transmission.

 2. Right to confront witnesses (select if the court advised you of your right to confront witnesses) 

• I have been advised that I have the right to confront in person and cross-examine any witnesses who testify in this proceeding.
•  I have been advised that I am not required to waive my right to in-person confrontation, and if I do not waive that right, this  

proceeding will be conducted in person and will not be unreasonably delayed as a result.

•  If represented by an attorney, I have had the opportunity to discuss my confrontation rights with my attorney and understand that I 

will be able to speak with my attorney privately during this proceeding.

•  I fully understand my right to in-person confrontation and appreciate the consequences of my decision to waive this right.
•  I therefore freely, voluntarily, and knowingly waive my right to in-person confrontation, and I consent to conducting this proceeding 

by audio and video transmission.
