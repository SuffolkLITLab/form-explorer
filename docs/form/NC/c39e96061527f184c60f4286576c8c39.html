I, the undersigned affiant, being first duly sworn, say that the paper-writing purporting to be the Last Will and Testament or codicil of the 
above-named decedent, which bears the date indicated above, is now offered for probate and was found after the testator’s death.

(complete for decedents dying on or before July 7, 2021) I further say that that paper-writing was found after the testator’s death

 among the testator’s valuable papers or effects. 

 in a safe deposit box or other safe place where it was deposited by the testator or under the testator’s authority.

  in the possession or custody of some person with whom, or some firm or corporation with which, it was deposited by the testator or 
under the testator’s authority for safekeeping.
