INSTRUCTIONS TO THE PETITIONER: 
1.  A copy of the Order of Paternity or Affidavit of Parentage MUST be attached to this Motion and Notice of Hearing.
2.   After receiving a hearing date from the clerk of superior court, you must serve a copy of this Motion and a Notice of Hearing on the 
other party to the Order of Paternity or the person who signed the Affidavit of Parentage. (NOTE: If you serve the Motion and Notice of 
Hearing by certified mail, you must prepare and file an affidavit of service.)

3.   You must serve the local child support enforcement agency if the agency has been involved in the establishment of paternity or the 

payment of child support for the child(ren) named in your motion.

4.  You will need to appear in court on the date and time provided in the Notice of Hearing.
5.   If you want the court to consider terminating your obligation to pay child support, you must file AOC-CV-671 (Motion And Notice Of 

Hearing For Relief From Child Support Obligation Due To Disestablishment Of Paternity).
