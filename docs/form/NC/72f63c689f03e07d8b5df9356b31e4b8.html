NOTE TO PETITIONER:
1.  This petition must be filed in the district court of the North Carolina county where you reside.

2.   Knowing and willful submission of false information on this petition is a Class 1 misdemeanor, and will result in a permanent prohibition from petitioning 

for restoration of firearms rights under G.S. 14-415.4.

3.   As part of this petition process, you must submit your fingerprints to the Sheriff for a State and national criminal background check by the State 

Bureau of Investigation (SBI). Contact the Sheriff of your county of residence in order to provide your fingerprints for that check. Print and complete 
the background check consent form at www.ncsbi.gov/SBI-Forms/SBIRestorationFirearmsRights.aspx, and provide it to the Sheriff when giving your 
fingerprints. The Clerk of Superior Court will not schedule this petition for a hearing until the results of that background check have been delivered to 
the Clerk’s office by the SBI.

4.   Upon delivery of the background check results, the Clerk will schedule this petition for hearing and will notify you and the district attorney of the hearing 

date.
