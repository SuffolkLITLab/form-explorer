NOTE: Use this form to compute balance of restitution still owed to the listed victims or aggrieved parties at revocation or termination of probation only 
when there is insufficient space to list them under the applicable Parts of the AOC-CR-612. Include the amounts found on this form in the total(s) of Part I 
on the AOC-CR-612. To calculate the balance due a victim/party, review restitution awards against other persons held jointly and severally liable to that 
victim/party with the defendant, and credit the total of all amounts paid to that victim/party pursuant to such awards toward the defendant's balance due.

Security Number or Tax ID number was not entered on the AOC-CR-611, record it on the AOC-CR-382 and deliver that form to 
bookkeeping.
