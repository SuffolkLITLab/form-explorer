INSTRUCTIONS: The Court should complete Part I. or Part II. of this form. Do not use this form for first-degree murder cases or murder cases where 
the degree is undesignated, except for the cases where the defendant was under 18 years of age at the time of the offense, or for capital post-conviction 
cases or appeals to the Court of Appeals or Supreme Court. For adult first-degree murder cases or murder cases where the degree is undesignated at the 
trial level, the Office of Indigent Defense Services will use form AOC-CR-624. For capital post-conviction cases, the Office of Indigent Defense Services will 
use form AOC-CR-625. For appellate cases, the Court will use form AOC-CR-350.
INDICACIONES: El juez debe completar la parte I. o la parte II. de este formulario. No utilice este formulario en causas por homicidio en primer grado 
ni en causas por homicidio cuyo grado no está designado, salvo cuando el acusado no tenía los 18 años en la fecha del delito o para causas capitales en 
la etapa pos condenatoria o para apelaciones al Tribunal de Apelación o al Tribunal Supremo. Para causas contra adultos por homicidio en primer grado
