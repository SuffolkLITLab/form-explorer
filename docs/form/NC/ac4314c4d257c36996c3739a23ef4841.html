NOTE:  This form provides a way to request that your social security, employer taxpayer identification, drivers license, state identification, 

passport, checking account, savings account, credit card, or debit card number, or personal identification (PIN) code or passwords be 
removed from an image or copy of an official record placed on a court’s/clerk of superior court’s internet website. If you are seeking to 
remove personal identifying information from an official record placed on a register of deeds’ internet website, do not use this form.

identifying information appears. It may be delivered to the clerk of superior court by mail, by fax, by electronic transmission, or by delivery in person. To find 
the mailing address, physical address, or telephone number for the clerk of superior court, visit www.nccourts.gov/locations, select a county, and visit the

be given the fax number. For counties where eCourts services are available, this form may be delivered to the clerk of superior court electronically by filing it
