INSTRUCTIONS:  Use this form when the applicant is a parent named in a juvenile petition to terminate the applicant’s parental rights or 
alleges abuse, neglect or dependency, or where the applicant’s appointed counsel was released but applicant is now 
party to a proceeding in which a motion to modify has been filed, or where the applicant is a parent of a juvenile who 
was placed in the custody of the department of social services after being adjudicated delinquent or undisciplined.
