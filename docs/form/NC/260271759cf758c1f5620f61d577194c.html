YOU ARE ORDERED TO APPEAR IN PERSON on the date and at the time and place shown below to show cause why

•  If the Court holds you in civil contempt, you may be committed to jail until you satisfy purge conditions set by the Court.

•  If the Court holds you in criminal contempt, you may be committed to jail for up to thirty (30) days, ordered to pay a ﬁne, 
  or both.
  Si el juez lo declara en desacato en la modalidad penal, es posible que se ordene su encarcelamiento por un máximo
