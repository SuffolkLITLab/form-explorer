   DISMISSAL WITH LEAVE

The undersigned prosecutor enters a dismissal with leave to the above offenses and assigns the following reasons:

  1.   The juvenile failed to appear for a proceeding at which the juvenile’s attendance was required and the prosecutor believes 

that the juvenile cannot readily be found.

  2.  Other: (specify)

NOTE:  This form must be completed and signed by the prosecutor when the dismissal occurs out of court. The better practice is for the prosecutor to 

complete and sign the form when the offenses are orally dismissed in open court.

 

 
In accordance with G.S. 7B-2404(b), the written dismissal must be served by the prosecutor on the juvenile, the juvenile’s parent, guardian, or 
custodian, and the juvenile’s counsel in cases where the dismissal occurred outside of court or where the prosecutor did not notify those parties 
of the dismissal in open court. Regardless of whether the dismissal occurred in open court or not, the written dismissal shall also be served on the 
chief court counselor or his or her designee, and, if the juvenile is being held in a detention center, the director of the detention center.
