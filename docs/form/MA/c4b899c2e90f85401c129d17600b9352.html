not comply with the provisions of Article II of the State Sanitary
Code, 105 C.M.R., §410.000;

On _____________________________, 200___, pursuant to §410.832 -

thereafter the defendant has failed to comply with the order, each
such day being a separate offense and a separate and distinct count
of this Complaint;

all in violation of State Sanitary Code, 105 C.M.R., §410.910-920,
and the public health law, Gen.L. c. 111, §127A, and the defendant
did so willfully, intentionally, recklessly or repeatedly.
