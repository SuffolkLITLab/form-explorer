The Court has received a petition, pursuant to G.L. c. 123, § 18(a½), requesting that the Prisoner, who is detained at the 
identified place of detention, be transferred to a suitable inpatient psychiatric facility or unit for the care and treatment of mentally 
ill persons licensed or operated by the Department of Mental Health, or to Bridgewater State Hospital.

The Court's findings are based upon (if necessary, use space below or attach additional pages):  

Information in the petition, supplements, and supporting materials.
Other information:

The Court ORDERS:

The petition is DENIED.
Additional Findings (optional):
The petition is ALLOWED, as the Prisoner       has been on mental health watch for at least 72 hours OR      is at "serious risk
of imminent and serious self-harm” OR      both. G.L. c. 123, § 18(a½)(2).
Additional Findings (optional):

The petition having been ALLOWED, the Prisoner shall be transferred for a period not to exceed 30 days to:

The following suitable inpatient facility for the care and treatment of mentally ill persons:
Bridgewater State Hospital, as the Court finds:

Facility

The Prisoner is male, and at the time of this Order, no suitable bed has been identified as available at a unit licensed 
or operated by the Department of Mental Health; or
The Prisoner is not a proper person for commitment to an inpatient psychiatric facility or unit licensed or operated by the
Department of Mental Health, and the failure to retain the Prisoner in strict custody would create a likelihood of serious 
harm.

During the 30-day period, a qualified physician or psychologist shall examine the Prisoner and report in writing their opinion, 
supported by clinical findings, as to whether the Prisoner needs further care and treatment by reason of mental illness at a facility 
or at Bridgewater State Hospital. Such report shall be sent to the court and to the person in charge of the place of detention. G.L. 
c. 123, § 18(a).

No later than the 30th day, the Prisoner shall be returned to the original place of detention unless a petition for further 
commitment has been filed in this Court pursuant to G.L. c. 123, § 18(a) or any other statutory provision. See G.L. c. 123, §§ 6(a), 
18(d). If the Prisoner is returned to the place of detention before the 30-day period expires, the place of hospitalization shall notify 
the Court. See G.L. c. 123, § 4.

The Court ORDERS that the Prisoner be transported to the place of hospitalization by officers of the custodial agency named in 
the Prisoner's mittimus who shall make a return of this Order to the Clerk-Magistrate of this Court. The officers shall transport the 
Prisoner to the original place of detention by                                              (not to exceed 30 days from the date of this Order and 
not to exceed the expiration of any sentence), or sooner upon request of the place of hospitalization, unless the place of detention 
has been notified that a petition has been filed in this Court for the commitment of the Prisoner. See G.L. c. 123, §§ 4, 6(a), 18(d).

The Court further ORDERS that, if the Prisoner is serving a sentence, the Department of Correction shall enter the expiration 
date of the Prisoner's sentence in the Prisoner's patient record. G.L. c. 123, § 18(c).
