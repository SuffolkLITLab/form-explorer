The filer may complete and file this Complaint form when language in a land record claims to forbid or restrict the transfer 
or use of property based on race, color, religion, national origin or sex.  There is no filing fee due for filing this Complaint.

• Attach to this Complaint: (1) a copy of the current (or, if not prepared, the last prepared) certificate of title (if registered 

land) or the most recent deed (if recorded land) and (2) a copy of the document or instrument that contains the challenged 
language.

File this Complaint with the Land Court Recorder’s Office at 3 Pemberton Square, Boston, MA 02108.  The court may 
order relief forthwith, require parties to be served with notice of the case, or hold a hearing.  The court may order the entry 
of a declaratory judgment, a new or amended certificate of title, the entry or cancellation of a memorandum upon a 
certificate of title, or any other relief it considers proper.
