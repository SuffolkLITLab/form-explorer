Instructions for Preparing Affidavit and Search Warrant

*Alternate procedures may be required for these items when using electromagnetic means for issuing warrants.

In paragraph one FULLY describe the person, place, or thing to be searched and give its EXACT location.

In paragraph three set forth the facts and observations that establish probable cause.  If additional pages are necessary,
continue on form MC 231a.
