DC 43   (3/15)   NOTICE OF SEIZURE AND INTENT TO FORFEIT AND DISPOSE OF PROPERTY

MCL 600.4704

In the case of personal property, the seizing agency is responsible for serving this notice.

In the case of real property, the attorney general, prosecuting attorney, or city/township attorney is responsible for serving this
notice.

This notice must be served within 28 days from the date of seizure or filing of lien notice.
