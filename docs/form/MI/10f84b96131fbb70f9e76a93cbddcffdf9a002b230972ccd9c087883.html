ŁIT IS ORDERED: INSTRUCTIONSDefinitionsŁA periodic garnishment lets the plaintiff (creditor) take money from a source that pays you on a regular basis such as yourearnings or income from rental properties. ŁA "garnishee" is a person who has control over some or all of the money that is paid to the defendant. For example, an employer could be a garnishee. ŁPeriodic payments are payments made by the garnishee to the defendant on a regular basis. These payments could be paychecks, rent payments, land contract payments, or other contract payments. In order to collect on this judgm ent, income to be paid to you may be garnished. 2.
