45-2-301 and 302(3), it is a crime for any person to aid and abet a crime, or, not being present, to advise and encourage a 
crime.  Under Montana Code Annotated § 45-2-303, any person who counsels, aids, solicits or incites another to commit a 
misdemeanor is guilty of a misdemeanor.   Therefore, it may be a crime for any person to encourage or invite contact 
between the Respondent and the Petitioner, except such contact as is expressly permitted by the above order.
