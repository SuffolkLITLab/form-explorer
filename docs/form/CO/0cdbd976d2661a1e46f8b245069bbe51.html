4. 

Some Options 

•  As noted above, you have the right to cure noncompliance for some grounds of 

termination within 90 days.  See C.R.S. § 38-12-203(1)(a), (1)(c), and (3). 

This time to cure runs at the same time as the requirement to sell or remove your 

mobile home from the premises.  You must pay rent and comply with other tenant 

obligations during this period, and acceptance of rent during this period does not 

waive the landlord's right to terminate the tenancy for noncompliance. 

•  You have the option of resolving this eviction through mediation under the Mobile 

Home Park Act or by filing a complaint with the Mobile Home Park Oversight 

Program.  Visit: cdola.colorado.gov/mobile-home-park-oversight for more 

information.  See C.R.S. §§ 38-12-216 and -1104.
