5.  You are presumed innocent unless proven guilty.
6.  The right to have each and every allegation in the contempt complaint proven by a preponderance of the

8.  The right to present all relevant witnesses and evidence you wish at trial or hearing.
9.  The right to request the Court to issue subpoenas to compel witnesses to appear and give testimony.

11.  The right to testify on your own behalf. If you testify, you waive your right against self-incrimination and

the prosecutor may cross-examine you.
