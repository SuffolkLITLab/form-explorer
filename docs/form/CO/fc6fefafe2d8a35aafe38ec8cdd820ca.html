This process confirms who the parents are when using assisted reproduction. 

Both parents must complete and file the Petition together. 

The parents may file with the District/Juvenile Court in: 

o  The county where the child was born or conceived. 
o  The county where either parent lives. 
o  Any county, if the child was born or conceived in Colorado. 

If the child was born in a Colorado hospital, the parents may have filled out a 

form to obtain an Acknowledgement of Parentage.  If so, you may attach the 

Acknowledgment to the Petition. 

The law that directs this process is C.R.S. § 19-5-203.5.
