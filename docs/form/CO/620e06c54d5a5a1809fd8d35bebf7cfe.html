This process asks the court to register a custody determination you got in another 

state.  And due to an emergency situation, you need those orders enforced now. 

The law that directs this process is C.R.S. § 14-13-305 and 308. 

You normally start the case in: 

The county and state where one or both parents live.  Or, 

The county and state where the child is present. 

This process isn’t for everyone.  See JDF 1215 if you are looking to register: 

1)  An alimony or child support order. 

2)  A divorce decree without a custody order. 

3)  A custody order without the need for expedited enforcement.
