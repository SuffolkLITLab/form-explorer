You must have an existing court order concerning parenting time. 

For Motions to Restrict Parenting time, check with the clerk’s office for 

special procedures. 

•  Generally, you must wait two years to file another request to change who the 

child is with the majority of the time. 

See JDF 1407 i to request a change in the child’s residence (big moves). 

Consider if your request change will modify child support. See JDF 1403 i for 

more info. 

If you file a motion the Court finds frivolous, groundless, or vexatious, you 

may have to pay the other parent’s attorney fees and costs. 

The law that directs this process is C.R.S. § 14-10-129.
