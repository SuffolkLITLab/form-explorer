This process asks the court to evict a tenant from a residential property. 

The Court can also award money for property damage or for back-due rent. 

A case can only be started by a Person of Interest.  For example, a property 

owner or someone identified in the lease. 

The case is started in the county where the property is located.  There is a 

County Court and a District Court in each county. 

The case is typically filed in the County Court.  But, if the requested 

judgment is over $25,000, then it must be filed in the District Court. 

You must file a written response to any claim or counterclaim.  Otherwise, 

the Court will enter a judgment against you. 

The law that directs this process is C.R.S. § 13-40-101 et seq.
