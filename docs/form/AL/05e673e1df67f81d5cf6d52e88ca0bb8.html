As  provided  by  Section  32-5A-191(c)  and  Sections  12-23-1  through  12-23-19, 
Code of Alabama, 1975, the Administrative Director of Courts is authorized to contract 
with agencies or entities to  conduct the court  referral education programs in each court 
jurisdiction  of  the  state.    At  this  time,  the  Administrative  Office  of  Courts  (AOC)  is 
soliciting  applications  for  an  education  program  provider  to  cover  Madison  County  in 
compliance with ยง12-23-4(a), Code of Alabama, 1975.   

It is the policy of the AOC to carefully select agencies to replace those that are no 
longer providing court referral services.  Consideration will be given to existing certified 
court referral education programs and other programs that are legally incorporated, non-
profit  alcohol/drug  abuse  or  governmental  agencies  existing  within  the  counties  to  be 
served.    In  the  event  that  no  qualified  agency  exists  or  is  willing  to  assume  these 
responsibilities,  other  appropriate  agencies  in  neighboring  counties  will  be  contacted  to 
determine the feasibility of their providing the services. 

If your agency is interested in providing the education services for these counties, 
you  must  submit  a  completed  application  and  checklist  to  this  office  no  later  than 
January 7, 2011.  Incomplete applications will not be accepted.  Enclosed you will find 
an application form and checklist.  If you have questions, do not hesitate to contact Millie 
Fuller at 1-866-954-9411, extension 5198.
