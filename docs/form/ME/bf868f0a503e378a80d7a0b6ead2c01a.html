MAINE TREATMENT COURT 

PROHIBITED SUBSTANCES AGREEMENT 

 
All positive alcohol and/or drug tests are considered a violation of program rules.  I agree to refrain 
from using the following foods or products that may potentially affect with the Maine Treatment Court 
testing procedures.  I understand that I am responsible for what I put into my body and that failure to abide 
by this policy may result in sanctions. 

oppy Seeds:   Poppy seeds are in the opioid family and may interact with urine drug testing.  I agree not 

to consume this item or other foods/ingestible products that contain this item. 

iet Pills:   Many appetite suppressants, weight loss medications, and supplements contain amphetamines 
or amphetamine-based substances that may interact with drug testing.  I agree to refrain from unapproved 
use of any appetite suppressant, weight loss medications, and supplements. 

llergy or Cold Medications:   Many over the counter and prescribed allergy or cold medications contain 
substances in the opioid family and amphetamine family and may interact with urine drug testing.  I agree 
to refrain from unapproved use of any allergy or cold medication. 

ough Syrups and other Liquid Medications:   I am aware that liquid cough syrup brands and numerous 
other liquid medications contain alcohol and may contain substances in the amphetamine family.  I will 
read product labels carefully and get permission before using.  I am aware that there are non-alcohol cough 
and cold remedies readily available at most pharmacies and major retail stores. 

on-Alcoholic  Beer  and  Wine:      Although  legally  considered  non-alcoholic,  I  acknowledge  that  non-
alcoholic beers such as O’Doul’s or Sharps do contain a residual amount of alcohol and I will refrain from 
using them. 

ood  and  Other  Ingestible  Products:      I  know  that  there  are  numerous  other  consumable  products  that 
contain alcohol that could result in a positive test result.  I agree to read the labels carefully and avoid using 
any products that contain alcohol. 

and  Sanitizers  and  Hygiene  Products:      Hand  sanitizers  and  other  antiseptic  gels  and  foams  used  to 
disinfect hands contain alcohol.  I understand that excessive, unnecessary, or repeated use of these products 
cannot be claimed as an excuse for a positive urine test.  I understand that use of these products may interfere 
with a SCRAM alcohol monitoring device.  I agree to avoid using these products, including aftershaves, 
colognes, hairsprays, mousses, astringents, and insecticides that contain alcohol. 

egal Substances/Medications:   I understand that just because a substance can be purchased, consumed, or 
used legally, such as alcohol, marijuana, cbd, kratom, synthetic or designer drugs, or narcotic medications, 
I  am  prohibited  from  possessing,  ingesting,  or  using  such  substances  while  participating  in  a  Maine 
Treatment Court.
