employer, or any change in the health insurance policy information, the obligor shall notify the Department of the change. Failure to 
report  a  change  of  address  or  employer  to  the  Department  within  15  days  of  such  change  is  a  civil  violation  for  which  a 
forfeiture not to exceed $200 may be adjudged for each violation.

order was issued or modified, the party must prove a substantial change in circumstances.
