B. THEREFORE, THE COURT MAKES THE FOLLOWING ORDERS:

Respondent:  These orders apply right now to you and anyone acting for you.  Read carefully.

1. You must go to court on (date)
2. Do not threaten, physically abuse or psychologically abuse the Petitioner or anyone living

with the Petitioner.

3. Do  not  contact,  write,  telephone,  or  otherwise  electronically  contact  (recorded  message,
pager, email, text message, instant message, etc.) the Petitioner or anyone living with the
Petitioner.

a. Attorneys for the Petitioner and Respondent may contact each other.

4. Do not approach or come within 100 feet of the Petitioner.

a. Petitioner and Respondent  may have LIMITED contact only for the purposes of
attending  courtroom  proceedings;  or  for  service  of  legal  documents  by  mail  or
through a process server.

5. Do not visit or approach within 100 yards of any place where the Petitioner lives or works
or goes to school.  Do not violate this order even if the Petitioner invites you to be at the place
where the Petitioner lives or works or goes to school.

6. FIREARMS  RESTRICTIONS.      Pursuant  to  section  134-7(f)  of  the  Hawai‘i  Revised
Statutes,  you,  Respondent,  and/or  anyone  acting  on  your  behalf,  are  prohibited  from
possessing, controlling, or transferring ownership of any firearm, ammunition, or firearm
permit or license for the duration of this Order or extension thereof.  All firearm permits or
licenses are hereby revoked.   You, Respondent, shall  immediately turn over all firearms,
ammunition, permits, and/or licenses to a police officer, nearest police station or to the Hawaii
Police Department (Firearms Unit, 349 Kapiolani  Street, Hilo, Hawaii), for the duration of
this Order or extension thereof.

7. Do not damage or otherwise disturb the property of the Petitioner or the property of anyone

living with the Petitioner.
