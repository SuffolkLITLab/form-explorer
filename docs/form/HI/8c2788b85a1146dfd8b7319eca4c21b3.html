Notice to Attend Kids First Prog.  1F-P-787 

DOCKET CODE: KIDS

KIDS FIRST INSTRUCTIONS 

IDS FIRST is a program designed to help parents understand the effects of separation and divorce on their children 
and to help children cope with changes in the families.  BOTH parents must attend.  Children (age 6-17) of either 
Plaintiff/Petitioner  or  Defendant/Respondent,  from  this  and  any  other  relationship  who  have  lived  with 
Plaintiff/Petitioner’s  and  Defendant/Respondent’s  current  family  must  attend  Kids  First  unless  excused  by  the 
Judge. 

ailure  to  attend  may  cause  you  to  be  brought  before  the  Court  to  explain  your  non-attendance  and  may  be 
considered  as  a  factor  in  custody  decisions.    Non-attendance  may  also  delay  the  granting  of  your  Paternity 
Judgment or Divorce Decree. 

ou and your children will watch a video.  Parents will have their own program presented by psychologists who 
specialize in paternity and divorce.  The children and teens will be grouped by age and led by trained facilitators.  
They  will  play  games,  see  the  courtroom,  role  play,  and  may  express  their  feelings  about  the  paternity  case  or 
divorce. 

OR PARENTS’ CLASS  

The Parents’ class is online only.  You will have Monday through Friday on the week of your scheduled class to 
complete the program.  Visit our website www.kidsfirsthawaii.com and click on Online Program.  Parents videos are 
on the left hand side.  After watching all videos, please complete the Feedback Form at the bottom of the page.  
Submit completed forms to: kidsfirsthawaii@gmail.com.  You must submit your forms by Friday in order to receive 
credit for attending your class.   Please keep in mind that early and late submissions will NOT be accepted.  If you 
have any future questions about the parents’ class, please contact our office at (808)954-8280. 

OR KIDS’ CLASS 

The Kids’ class is a virtual interactive 1-hour class via Zoom.  It will be held on Tuesday, Wednesday, or Thursday 
evening depending on their age.  These Zoom classes will NOT be recorded due to confidentiality of all minor aged 
children in the class.  Please do not record your child’s Kids First session as any recordings will not be submissible 
in court.  The Kids First staff will be calling you to give you more information regarding the Kids’ Zoom classes. 
 

You must call (808) 954-8280 if any of the following apply: 
•  Child Protective Service Case 
•  Child with special needs (speech, vision, hearing, intellectually impaired) 
•  You received this notice after your scheduled date 
• 
If you need an interpreter 
•  Any scheduling questions 

Leave your name, daytime telephone/cell phone number and a detailed message on the recording on (808) 
954-8280.  Your call will be returned as soon as possible.
