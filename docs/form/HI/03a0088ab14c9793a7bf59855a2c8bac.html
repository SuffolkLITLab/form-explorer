INSTRUCTION SHEET FOR NON-HEARING MOTION
FOR CHANGE OF VENUE (TRAFFIC CRIME/CRIME);
AND CERTIFICATE OF SERVICE

WHEN SHOULD THIS FORM BE USED:

The Non-Hearing Motion for Change of Venue (Traffic Crime/Criminal) may be used
pursuant to the Hawai‘i Rules of Penal Procedure (“HRPP”) to request a transfer of
venue if all of the following apply:

1. Your case is a District Court Traffic Crime or Criminal case only.  This form is not

to be used for Traffic Infraction or Civil cases.

2. You seek to transfer your case to another circuit.  This form should not be used
to request transfer of a case from one division to another division in the same
circuit.  For example, do not use this form to request transfer of the case from
the Honolulu Division to the ‘Ewa Division since both courts are with in the same
circuit.  The District Court of each circuit is listed below:

a. District Court of the First Circuit – O‘ahu
b. District Court of the Second Circuit – Includes Maui, La- na‘i, Moloka‘i
c. District Court of the Third Circuit – Hawai‘i
d. District Court of the Fifth Circuit – Kaua‘i

3. You have not yet appeared for arraignment (see HRPP Rule 22).

HOW DO I MAKE SURE THAT MY MOTION IS COMPLETED?

1. The form should be typed or printed clearly in black or blue ink.  Do not leave

any item blank.

2. Fill in your name, mailing address, city, state, zip code, and telephone

number(s).  The court’s response will be mailed back to you at your mailing
address.

3. Fill in the name of the circuit (i.e. First, Second, Third, or Fifth) that the case has

been filed in.

4. Fill in the division (for example, Honolulu, Ka‘u-, La-na‘i, etc.) you are scheduled
to appear in.  This is on your Citation for Traffic Crime Arrest (“CTCA”), or court
document for the case.

5. Fill in your name in the parties caption on the line above the word “Defendant.”

12. You must complete a separate motion for each citation number or Criminal Case

13. Make a minimum of 6 copies for each motion that you submit for filing.  Submit

the original and copies for filing.  Keep a copy for yourself.

14. When you receive the filed copies back from the clerk, you must serve the filed

copies of the motion on:

the originating circuit’s prosecuting attorney, or if applicable, attorney

b. any affected parties (for example, co-defendants, or their counsels).

If you are not filing the Motion in the originating circuit, a $10 filing fee is required.

WHAT HAPPENS NEXT?

After reviewing your motion, the court will issue an Order Re: Non-Hearing Motion for
Change of Venue (Traffic Crime/Crime) which will be mailed to the address you
provided on the Non-Hearing Motion for Change of Venue (Traffic Crime/Criminal).
