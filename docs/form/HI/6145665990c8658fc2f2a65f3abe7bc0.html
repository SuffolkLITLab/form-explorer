2F-P-362 

FIREARMS PROHIBITION 

 

 
IT IS ORDERED that you will not possess or control any firearm(s) or ammunition.  All permits/licenses 
are hereby revoked.  You must surrender any firearm(s), ammunition, permits and/or licenses that you presently 
possess or control to any police officer that serves this order.  If you are the registered owner of a firearm, you 
must tell the police officer who serves this order where all such firearms are located.  Failure to surrender or to 
disclose the location of a firearm is a misdemeanor. 

 

If you are able to furnish proof to the police officer serving this order that you are a law enforcement 

 
officer, sheriff, member of the armed forces of the State of Hawaii or United States, a mail carrier, or employed 
by the State of Hawaii or a subdivision thereof, or of the United States and you are required to be armed while 
on duty, the restrictions do not apply to you to the extent  that while you are required to be armed, you may 
possess or control firearms and ammunition which are official equipment but only while on duty and required to 
be armed.  You shall not possess or control any firearms, ammunition, firearms permits and/or licenses when not 
on duty nor any personal firearms while on duty. 

Failure to comply with this Firearms Prohibition is a violation of HRS Section 134-7(f).
