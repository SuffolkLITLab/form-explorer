Court in an adoption proceeding by the proposed adoptive parent(s), this consent may not be withdrawn 
or repudiated without the written order of a judge who has jurisdiction in adoption proceedings, based 
upon a written finding supported by proof to be submitted by me, that the withdrawal of my consent 
would be in the best interest of the child(ren).
