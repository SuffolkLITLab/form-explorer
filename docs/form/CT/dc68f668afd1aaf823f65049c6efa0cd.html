In the Matter of 

 

The respondent has active tuberculosis or is suspected of having active tuberculosis. (State factual basis.) 

The following efforts have been made to educate and counsel the respondent about the need for examination and the 
medical and legal consequences of failing to agree to it:  

The respondent is unable or unwilling to submit voluntarily to an examination. (State factual basis.) 

A Probate Court order of examination of the respondent for active tuberculosis is necessary and is the least restrictive 
alternative to protect the public health. 

The respondent is unable to obtain an attorney and requests that the court appoint an attorney to represent the respondent. 

The respondent is represented by an attorney, who has or will file an appearance in the court. 

WHEREFORE, the petitioner requests: 

The Probate Court order and authorize the respondent's removal to, and detention in, the following facility or setting 
for the purpose of facilitating examination of the respondent by a physician for tuberculosis: (Name, address and 
telephone number)  

The Probate Court issue a warrant for the apprehension of the respondent to deliver the respondent to the 
above facility or setting to submit to an examination by a physician for tuberculosis. 

The representations made in this petition are made under penalty of false statement.
