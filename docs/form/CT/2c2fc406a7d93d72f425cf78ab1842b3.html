      Instructions:

1)  A trustee of a testamentary trust may use this form to file an inventory of the estate assets.  A 

“testamentary trust” is defined under C.G.S. section 45a-499c(28) as a “trust created under a will and, 
unless expressly provided, any trust established pursuant to an order of the Probate Court.”   

2) The fiduciary is required to file an initial inventory within two months from the date of appointment.
3) List real property (and attach a complete copy of the recorded deed) and personal property in the
manner described. List trust property using acquisition value as defined in Probate Court Rules of
Procedure, section 36.14(a)(2).

4) The fiduciary must send a copy of the inventory to each party and attorney and certify to the court that

a copy has been sent.

5) For more information, see C.G.S. section 45a-340 et seq.
6) Type or print in ink. Use an additional sheet, or PC-180, if more space is needed.
