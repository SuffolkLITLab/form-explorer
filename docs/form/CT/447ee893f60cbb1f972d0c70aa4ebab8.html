Appointment of Probate Judge 
as Agent for Service by  
Non-Resident Fiduciary 
PC-482 REV. 7/19 

RECEIVED: 

C O N N E C T I C U T   P R O B A T E  C O U R T S  

      Instructions: 

1)  A proposed fiduciary who resides outside the State of Connecticut or an appointed fiduciary who is 

moving out of Connecticut uses this form to appoint the probate judge as agent for service of process.  
This form must be signed and acknowledged before an officer authorized to take acknowledgements in 
the state in which the form is signed.  

2) For further information, see C.G.S. sections 52-60 and 52-61.
3) Type or print in ink.

Probate Court Name

Estate of/In the Matter of 

Fiduciary (Name and address) 

District Number 

Date 

Position of Trust 

I have been proposed/appointed to the position of trust in the above estate or matter. 

I am a non-resident of Connecticut residing at the above address and hereby appoint the judge for the above probate 
district, and his or her successors in office, to be my agent upon whom all process may be served in any action or 
proceeding against me in my representative capacity and in any garnishment of the estate in my possession as fiduciary.  
I also appoint the probate judge to be my agent for service of all process in any action against me in my individual 
capacity founded upon or arising from my acts or omissions as fiduciary.  

I hereby agree that any process served on the probate judge or his or her successors in office shall be of the same force 
and validity as if served upon me personally, subject to the applicable provisions of the statutes of Connecticut. This 
appointment shall continue in force as long as any liability remains outstanding against me as fiduciary and as long as the 
estate is in my possession as fiduciary.   

In witness whereof, I hereby execute this instrument on the date written above. 

State of 

County of 

Fiduciary 

On this ___   day of __________________, _____  , before me, the undersigned officer, personally appeared the above-
named fiduciary known to me (or satisfactorily proven) to be the person described in the foregoing instrument and 
acknowledged that he/she executed the same in the capacity therein stated and for the purposes therein contained.  

Appointment of Probate Judge as Agent for Service by Non-Resident Fiduciary 

PC-482 

Judge, Clerk, Notary Public, Comm. of Superior Court
