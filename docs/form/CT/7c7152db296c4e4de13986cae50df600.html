Instructions:  
Complete this form and have it notarized.  Attach: (1) a Financial Affidavit (form JD-FM-6-SHORT or JD-FM-6-LONG) 
from each petitioner, (2) Notice of Automatic Orders - Nonadversarial Divorce (form JD-FM-260), and (3) a completed 
Appearance (form JD-CL-12) from each petitioner. If you have a settlement agreement (form JD-FM-243) that you are 
asking the court to include in the divorce decree, you must also file that agreement with this joint petition. After you file 
this form and before the court orders a divorce decree, if either petitioner wants to stop this nonadversarial process, a 
petitioner must file with the clerk a Notice of Revocation (form JD-FM-245).
