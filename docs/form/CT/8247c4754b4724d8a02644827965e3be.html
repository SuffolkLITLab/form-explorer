C O N N E C T I C U T   P R O B A T E  C O U R T S  

      Instructions: 

1) If a conservator of the estate, or a fiduciary with equivalent authority, has been appointed in another state 
for an incapable person who is not domiciled in Connecticut, a family member of the conserved person or 
a conservator or guardian of the estate or person of the incapable person may use this form to petition for 
appointment of a conservator of the estate with authority over Connecticut real or tangible personal 
property owned by the conserved person.   

2) The petition, together with a certified copy of the appointment of a conservator of the estate in the state
of the incapable person’s domicile, must be filed in the court for the probate district in which the real or
tangible personal property, in whole or in part, is situated. Also file Confidential Sheet, PC-3011CI, with
the respondent's confidential social security number.
Contact the court regarding payment of service of process fees.

3)
4) For more information, see C.G.S. section 45a-659.
5)
