 CONFIDENTIAL 

 Instructions: 

1) The sole parent, a statutory parent or a guardian of the person of a minor child who is free for adoption as 
provided in C.G.S. sections 45a-724 and 45a-725 may use this form to petition for approval of an adoption 
of the minor child. 

2) The petition must be filed in duplicate (unless filed electronically) in the court for the probate district in

which the adoptive parent resides or the district where the statutory parent is located.

3) The following documents must be filed with the petition: a) certified long-form birth certificate, b)
Adoption Agreement, PC-681, c) Adoption Data Sheet, PC-680, d) JD-FM-164, Affidavit Concerning
Children, and e) Confidential Sheet, PC-603CI, with confidential social security numbers.

4) For more information, see C.G.S. sections 45a-724 et seq. and Rule 40 of the Probate Court Rules of

Procedure.

Probate Court Name
