      Instructions: 

1) An adoptive parent or a duly authorized officer of a child-placing agency may use this form to petition 

the court to validate an adoption of a minor child born outside of the United States or its territories when 
the adoption was finalized in a jurisdiction other than the United States or its territories, and the minor 
child is unable to obtain United States citizenship because the adoptive parents did not personally see 
and observe the child prior to or during the adoption proceedings.   

2) The petition must be filed in the probate district in which the petitioner resides or in the district in which
an office of the child-placing agency is located. The petition shall be accompanied by an authenticated
and exemplified copy of the adoption unless the court waives the requirement for good cause. A certified
or authenticated copy of the birth certificate for the minor child should also be submitted.

3) For more information, see C.G.S. section 45a-730.
4) Type or print the form in ink.
