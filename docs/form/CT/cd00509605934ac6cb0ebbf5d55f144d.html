      Instructions: 

1) A child who is under the age of 16 and hospitalized for evaluation or treatment of a mental disorder under 

an emergency or diagnostic certificate as provided under C.G.S. section 17a-78 (a), or the child’s 
representative, may use this form to request a Probate Court hearing to determine if there is probable 
cause to conclude that the child is subject to involuntary hospitalization pursuant to C.G.S. section 17a- 78. 

2) The child, or the child’s representative, should submit the form to the head of the hospital.
3) The hospital shall immediately file the request in the court of probate for district in which the hospital is
located.
4) For more information, see C.G.S. sections 17a-78 and Rule 44 of the Probate Court Rules of  Procedure.
5) Type or print the form in ink.
