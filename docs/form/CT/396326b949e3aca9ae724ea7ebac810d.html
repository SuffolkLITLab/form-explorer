      Instructions:  

1)  A person authorized to give a minor in adoption under C.G.S. section 45a-724 may enter into an 
adoption agreement with the adopting parent or parents if the minor is free for adoption.  
2) Both the person giving the minor in adoption and the adopting parent or parents must sign the

adoption agreement. 

3) This adoption agreement should be filed in duplicate along with Form PC-601, Petition/Consent
Termination of Parental Rights and Stepparent, Co-Parent or Relative Adoption, or PC-603, 
Petition/Adoption.  

4) For more information, see C.G.S. sections 45a-724, 45a-725 and 45a-727.
5) Type or print the form in ink. Use a separate sheet, or PC-180, Second Sheet, if more space is needed.
