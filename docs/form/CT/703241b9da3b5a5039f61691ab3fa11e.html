established a relationship with the minor. (Include a description of proposed guardian’s relationship with minor.) 

The minor consents to the appointment or continuation of a guardian after attaining the age of 18. 

The guardian and minor have filed a petition for special immigrant juvenile findings pursuant to C.G.S. section 45a-608n. 

The appointment of the proposed guardians of the person of the minor is in the best interests of the minor.        

THEREFORE, the petitioner requests that: 
The court appoint the proposed guardians listed above as guardians of the person of the minor, the petitioner having
