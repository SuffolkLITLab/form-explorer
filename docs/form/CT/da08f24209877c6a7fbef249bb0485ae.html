      Instructions: 

1) A person having an interest in an estate may use this form to commence an action in the Probate Court 
against a surety of a probate bond to recover for losses incurred because a fiduciary failed to perform 
properly the fiduciary duties.  An award for damages cannot exceed the amount of the probate bond.  
2) If the petitioner is filing this action in the petitioner’s own right alone and not as a representative of the
estate or on behalf of all persons having an interest in the estate, provide the name, address and interest
or rights of all other persons who may have an interest in the action.

3) For more information, see C.G.S. section 45a-144 and C.G.S. section 52-117 and the Probate Court Rules of

Procedure, section 35.12.

4) Type or print the form in ink. Use an additional sheet, or PC-180, if more space is needed.
