      Instructions: 

1) The commissioner of social services may use this form to petition the court for an order authorizing the 

commissioner or his designees to enter the premises of an elderly person for the sole purpose of an 
assessment when the commissioner has reasonable cause to believe that the elderly person may be in 
need of protective services, but the department has been denied access by the elderly person or another 
individual. 

2) The petition should be filed in the court for the probate district in which the elderly person resides or is

located at the time of filing the petition.

3) For more information, see C.G.S. section 17b-464.
4) Type or print the form in ink.  Use an additional sheet if more space is needed.

name and address of each person.)
