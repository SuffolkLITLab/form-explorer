TO THE CITIZENS OF SOUTH DAKOTA: 

 
 The South Dakota Constitution divides the powers of our governme nt among three 
independent branches: the executive branch led by the Governor; the legislative branch led by the 
President of the Senate and Speaker of the House; and the judicial branch supervised by the Supreme 
Court. 

The South Dakota Judiciary safeguards the rights of every citizen and assures due process of 

 
law.  Each of us in the Unified Judicial System assumes a special responsibility to ensure that our 
South Dakota courts are accessible and responsive to the state and its citizens for, ultimately, it is you 
the public that we serve. 

It is with pride and pleasure that we present this outline and description of your South Dakota 

 
court system.
