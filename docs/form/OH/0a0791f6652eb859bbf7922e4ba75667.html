Instructions for Completing Form A 
Supplement for Commercial Dockets

Each judge assigned to hear commercial 
docket cases shall submit to the Case 
Management Section of the Supreme 
Court of Ohio each month a copy of this 
Form A Supplement for Commercial 
Dockets pursuant to the general reporting 
requirements prescribed under Sup. R. 
37 and in accordance with the 
Instructions for Preparation of All Report 
Forms.  

All cases assigned to a commercial 
docket judge shall retain their originating 
case type classification and shall 
continue to be reported by the 
Commercial Docket Judge in their 
traditional Form A report.  Accordingly, 
the case counts reported in this Form A 
Supplement for Commercial Dockets will 
be a subset of the case counts reported 
in the judge's traditional Form A report.

Where this Form A Supplement for 
Commercial Dockets varies from the 
standard Form A, the following 
instructions apply:

1.  All cases assigned to a commercial 
docket judge during the reporting month 
shall be reported in Line 2 except for 
cases first assigned to another 
commercial docket judge and then 
transferred to the reporting judge during 
the reporting month in which instance 
they shall be reported in Line 3.  Cases 
previously terminated by the reporting 
judge that subsequently become 
reactivated shall be reported in Line 4.

2. Cases terminated by a court trial heard 
by a Special Master shall be reported in 
Line 8.

3. Cases terminated through mediation 
shall be reported in Line 9.
