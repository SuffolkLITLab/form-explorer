Rule to Modify Child Support 

his form (SES 886 – 08/20) is published by the Louisiana Department of Social Services “Support 

Enforcement Services.” 

. 

This  form  addresses  modification  (increase  or  decrease)  of  existing  child  support  judgments.  
Louisiana  law  requires  that  a  material  change  in  circumstances  exist  before  you  are  entitled  to  a 
modification.  As a general rule, the modification becomes effective from the date that the Rule is filed with 
the Court. This form applies when the modification is being filed in the parish and court where the prior 
child support judgment (which hasn’t been registered elsewhere), was rendered.
