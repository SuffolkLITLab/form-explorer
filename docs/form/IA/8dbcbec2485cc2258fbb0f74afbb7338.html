Instructions: 

•  Guardian must complete, sign, and file this form with the court within sixty (60) days of appointment.

8.37—Form 1: Protected Information Disclosure.

situation, Protected Minor’s needs, and Guardian’s plan to meet those needs. 
Provide as much detailed information as possible.
