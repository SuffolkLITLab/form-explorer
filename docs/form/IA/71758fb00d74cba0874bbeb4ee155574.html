Iowa Judicial Branch 
Request for an Electronic Recording of a Court Proceeding 
Instructions:  Complete this form (type or print clearly).  Take or mail it to the clerk of district court in the 
county where the hearing occurred.  If you mail this form, include a personal check (not cash) for the 
correct fee (in part D., below).   You will receive a recording on a DVD or other digital media that you can 
listen to on a computer. 

A.

I request a copy of an electronic recording of a court proceeding for the following case:*

1. County where the proceeding occurred:

2. Case number:

3. Date(s) of the court proceeding (month / day / year):

4. Name of the judge or magistrate who conducted the proceeding:

5. Name of the person requesting the recording:

* Mental health (MH) commitment and juvenile proceedings (excluding delinquency) are confidential.  If you are
requesting a copy of a recording of a confidential proceeding, you must complete a special request form, available at
the Clerk of District Court office.

B. The electronic recording should be delivered to me in the following manner (check one):
1. ☐  I will pick up the recording on DVD or USB flash/thumb drive at the clerk of court office.**
2. ☐  Deliver the recording to me by regular U.S. mail (USPS); there is a $7.00 fee for this service.

 If you check B.2, complete section C (below).
Note:  Delivery by email is not available.

C.

If you request delivery of the recording by U.S. mail, you must complete this section.
[This section is optional for all others.]

1. First and last names:

2. Street address:

3. City, State

4. Zip Code:

5. Telephone number:

6. Date (mo/day/year):

D. Recording fee: $10.00 for each day on which the court proceeding occurred (see A.3, above).

 Add $7.00 only for delivery of the recording via U.S. mail (if you checked B.2, above).

Total fee paid:  $ 

To be completed by clerk of district court personnel: 
Fee paid by: ☐  cash /  ☐  check /  ☐  waived 

Clerk’s initials: 

** The recording fee may be paid in cash (if you personally deliver it to the clerk’s office) or by personal check.  You 
must pay the fee when you submit this request to the clerk of court.  However, if your filing fee for this case was 
waived, you will not have to pay the recording fee.  The recording will be available for pick-up (or will be placed in the 
mail) within 7 work days after you submit this form and the recording fee to the clerk of district court.  NOTE:  If you 
choose to pick up the DVD or USB flash/thumb drive, you must pick it up within 10 days after the agreed upon pick-
up date.  Thereafter, the digital media will be erased and reused.
