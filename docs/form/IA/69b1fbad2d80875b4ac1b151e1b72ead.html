Rule 17.400—Form 410: Affidavit of Service of Original Notice and Petition for Custody and Visitation 

Petitioner:  Use this form only if Respondent did not sign an Acceptance of Service (form 405) or a person who is

•  Petitioner, or the person who gave the Petition and Original Notice to Respondent, must file this form with the clerk of court.
