24. Protected or confidential information 

 

This file is a public record available to anyone, pursuant to Iowa Code section 236.10.  If you 
would like all or part of this file not to be available to the general public in order to protect the 
safety or privacy of any person, then you must ask the court to “seal” all or part of the file.   

  Defendant from whom you are seeking protection will have access to the file, even if the court 
seals it.  Some court records cannot be sealed, such as court orders and support payment 
records.  The court upon request may remove address and location information from those 
records.
