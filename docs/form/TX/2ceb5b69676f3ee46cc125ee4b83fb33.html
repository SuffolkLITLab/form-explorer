ORDER DETERMINING PLAINTIFFS TO BE VEXATIOUS LITIGANTS 

On February 23, 2023, the Court heard Defendant HSBC Bank, USA’s Motion  for  Order 

Determining  Plaintiffs  to  be  Vexatious  Litigants  (the  “Motion”).    Having  considered the 

Motion, Plaintiffs’ response, if any, the evidence presented, and all other arguments of  counsel 

and  the  parties,  the  Court  finds  that  good  cause  exists  to  grant  the  Motion  in  its  entirety.  It  is 

therefore: 

Remedies Code; it is further 

ORDERED that Plaintiffs Jimmy Ray Sepeda and Hortencia Sepeda are both found and 

determined  to  be  vexatious  litigants  under  Chapter  11  of  the  Texas  Civil  Practice  and 

ORDERED that Plaintiffs Jimmy Ray Sepeda and Hortencia Sepeda are both prohibited 

from filing, pro se, any new litigation in any court in this state without seeking the permission 

of  the  appropriate  local  administrative  judge  as  detailed  in  Section  11.102  of  the  Texas  Civil 

Practice and Remedies Code; it is further
