Reynaldo  Morales' (collectively, "Defendants")  Motion to Declare Plaintiff Alejandro

Hernandez a Vexatious  Litigant  and  Request for Security and Motion for Prefiling  Order.

HERNANDEZwas  given  proper  notice  of the hearing  and appeared  pro se.

Civil  Practice and Remedies  Code.
