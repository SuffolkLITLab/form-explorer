THIRTEENTH COURT OF APPEALS 
SUMMER 2023 JUDICIAL INTERNSHIP PROGRAM 
 
The  Thirteenth  Court  of  Appeals  is  accepting  applications  for  its  Summer  2023  Judicial 
Internship Program from first, second, and third-year law students. The program consists of 
one  four-to-six  week  nonpaying  internship  which  will  run  from  Monday,  May  15,  2023, 
through Friday, June 23, 2023, in the Edinburg and Corpus Christi offices of the court. The 
program is currently intended to be held in-person at the court’s offices. Applications will be 
accepted until available positions are filled. 

Description:  Interns  work  as  part  of  a  team  under  the  supervision  of  a  justice,  a  senior 
attorney, and a junior staff or briefing attorney. Interns selected for the program will receive 
training  and  supervision  in  legal  research  and  writing  and  will  perform  hands-on  work  with 
the justices and attorneys of the court. Interns assist the justices with general legal research, 
analysis,  and  writing,  and  participate  in  case  conferences  with  the  court’s  justices  and 
attorneys. Interns may perform related work as required. 

Qualifications.  Applicants  must  possess  knowledge  of  legal  principles,  practices,  and 
proceedings, and skill in legal research, writing, and analysis. Applicants must be proficient 
in  computer  and  word-processing  skills  and  must  be  able  to  communicate  clearly  and 
effectively.  Strong  preference  is  given  to  candidates  with  outstanding  academic  records, 
demonstrated  writing  ability,  law  review  or  journal  experience,  moot  court  or  mock  trial 
experience, and prior work for an appellate court or other appellate experience. 

Application Process. Applicants should submit: (1) a cover letter; (2) resume; (3) law school 
transcript; (4) writing sample, if available; and (5) the names of two references or two letters 
of recommendation. Applicants should specify whether they are applying for an internship in 
Edinburg  or  Corpus  Christi  and  should  specify  if  they  would  like  their  application  to  be 
considered  by  the  entire  court  or  only  by  a  specific  justice  or  justices.  Please  email  the 
completed  packet  as  one  document  in  PDF  format  to  cecile.gsanger@txcourts.gov.  The 
submission of applications by email is preferred, but if you cannot email your application, the 
completed packet should be mailed to: Thirteenth District Court of Appeals, Attn: Chief Staff 
Attorney Cecile Foy Gsanger, Nueces County Courthouse, 901 Leopard Street, Tenth Floor, 
Corpus Christi, Texas 78401. Please call the court at (361) 888-0416 or Cecile Foy Gsanger 
at  (832)  922-5856  with  any  questions.  Incomplete  application  packets  may  not  be 
considered.
