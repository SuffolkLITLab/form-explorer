1.)  Parties must pay or make arrangements to pay for the reporter's record unless indigent.  

2.)  If a designation is not filed, the reporter is required to prepare a record according to TRAP 34.6(a) as well as the Uniform Format Manual for Texas 
      Reporters' Records.

3.)  In a criminal case in which a party requests a reporter's record, the court reporter must prepare a duplicate of the reporter's record and file it with the trial
       court clerk. TRAP 34.6(h)

4.)  In addition to the paper copy, the Court requests an electronic copy of the reporter's record on a CD with a searchable PDF format until you are able to
       electronically file them online through the portal for TAMES.

5.)  If you need additional time to prepare your record you will need to send in a request for extenion of time.  A form is available on our web page. *
       A copy of your monthly report pursuant to TRAP 13.4 must accompany your request for extension of time.
