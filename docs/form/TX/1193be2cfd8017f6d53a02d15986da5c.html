ORDERED that: 

1.

By order dated June 9, 2014, in Misc. Docket No. 14-9113, the Supreme Court of
Texas  approved  amendments  to  Rules  I,  II,  III,  XIII,  XIV,  XVII,  and  XIX  of  the  Rules 
Governing  Admission  to  the  Bar  of  Texas  and  invited  public  comment.  After  the  comment 
period expired, the Court made revisions to the rules. This order incorporates those revisions and 
contains the final version of the rules. The amendments are effective October 1, 2014.
