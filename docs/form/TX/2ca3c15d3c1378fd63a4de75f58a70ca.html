Rule 13. Court Reporters and Court Recorders 

13.5. Appointing Deputy Reporter 

When the official court reporter is unable to perform the duties in 13.1 or 13.2 because of illness, 
press  of  official  work,  or  unavoidable  absence  or  disability,  the  trial  court  may  designate  a 
deputy reporter.  If the court appoints a deputy reporter, that person must file with the trial court 
clerk a document stating: 

the date the deputy worked;

the court in which the deputy worked; and

the number and style of the case on which the deputy worked.; and

the deputy’s name, mailing address, telephone number, fax number, if any, email address,
and Certified Shorthand Reporter number.
