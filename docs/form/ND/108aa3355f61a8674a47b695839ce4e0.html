STARTING A CIVIL ACTION 
CHECKLIST 

This is a basic checklist for starting a civil action in North Dakota District Court.  There may be 
additional documents and procedures for your particular type of civil action.  If you are 
unsure how to proceed, consult a lawyer licensed to practice in North Dakota. 

THE GUIDE TO A CIVIL ACTION on the ND Legal Self Help Center website has additional 
information and resources. 

Jurisdiction: 

The Plaintiff(s) must show that North Dakota District Courts have subject matter jurisdiction 
and personal jurisdiction over the civil action and the parties in the civil action.
