READ BEFORE COMPLETING THE AMENDED JUDGMENT (PROPOSED) 

This Amended Judgment form is part of the Modifying Primary Residential 
Responsibility (Custody) with a Written Agreement forms packet.  

To complete this form, you must recreate your judgment by doing three things:

Copy the entire contents of each unamended paragraph of the judgment or
most recently amended judgment you’re asking the court to modify; and

When you come to the paragraphs you want to modify, copy and underline the
EXACT wording of the amended paragraph from your proposed order.

If you’re asking the court to add new paragraphs, copy and underline the EXACT
wording of each NEW paragraph from your proposed order.

You have two options to recreate your judgment using this form: 

Option One: Use the fillable form as is.  This form includes 10 blank pages in which you 
may type the words and formatting of your proposed amended judgment, including 
paragraph numbers.  If your proposed amended judgment is more than 10 pages in 
length, use Option Two. 

CAUTION: The ND Legal Self Help Center webpage DOESN’T automatically save 
what you’ve typed into a form.  Remember to save the form as you go, so you 
don’t lose what you’ve typed. 

Option Two: Use a word processing program.  Use a word processing program, such as 
Microsoft Word, WordPerfect, Google Docs, or OpenOffice, to type the words and 
formatting of your proposed amended judgment, including paragraph numbers. 

Copy and paste the ENTIRE first page of the form, EXCEPT the italicized paragraphs in 
(parentheses), into the first page of your document.  Then, copy and paste all of the 
words on the second page of the form into your document.   

IMPORTANT: Make sure the formatting of the words you copied and pasted are 
the same as the formatting in the amended judgment form. 

Then, type the words and formatting of your proposed amended judgment, including 
paragraph numbers.  Include the Clerk of Court date and signature block at the end. 

Don’t include this cover sheet when you serve or file the completed form.
