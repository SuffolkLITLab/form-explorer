CONCLUSIONS OF LAW 

There is an overriding interest in protecting the defendant from further harm by 

prohibiting public access to all of the case records in the above-captioned case. 

There is a minimal interest in maintaining public access to the case records in the above-

captioned case. 

The interest of justice will be served by prohibiting public access to all of the electronic 

case records in the above-captioned case. 

ORDER 

Based on the above, the clerk of court is ordered to promptly restrict public access to 

the all of the case records in the above-captioned case. 

The clerk of court is further ordered to promptly restrict public access to all of the 

electronic case records in the above-captioned case and to ensure that the search result for 

these records displays the words “Internet Access Prohibited under N.D.Sup.Ct.Admin.R 41.”
