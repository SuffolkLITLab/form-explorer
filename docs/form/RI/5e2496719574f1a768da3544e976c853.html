INSTRUCTIONS TO THE DEFENDANT/TENANT 

Listed below are several possible defenses to  the eviction  action  that the Plaintiff/Landlord

consult a lawyer and seek representation before filling out this Answer. If you receive a court 
date you must appear in court for the hearing.
