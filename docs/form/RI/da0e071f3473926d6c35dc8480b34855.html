______________________________________________________________________________

WHEREFORE, the Plaintiff asks this court to: 

1. Enter  a  Temporary  Restraining  Order  and  Preliminary  and  Permanent  Injunction,

enjoining the Defendant to: 

 Immediately give Plaintiff full access to and use of the rental premises and to return 

any property he/she may have removed from Plaintiff’s apartment. 

 Immediately restore all utility services that he/she terminated or interrupted. 

 Cease and desist from entering Plaintiff’s apartment without first giving Plaintiff 
proper notice and obtaining Plaintiff’s prior consent except in case of emergency. 

and 

2. Award the Plaintiff statutory damages as allowed by  G.L. 1956 §§ 34-18-34 and/or 34-

18-45 and grant such further relief as this court deems fit and proper.
