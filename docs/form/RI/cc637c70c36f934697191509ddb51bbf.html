Case Number

Defendant   
 

IT IS HEREBY AGREED that pursuant to the provisions of G.L. 1956 § 12-19-19, sentence 
may be deferred on the above-referenced case for a period of               years as long as the court is 
satisfied the defendant has kept the peace, has been of good behavior, and has not violated any of 
the terms of this agreement as provided below. 

I, _____________________________________________  AGREE TO THE FOLLOWING:  

1.  I will not violate any criminal law of  the United States, the State of Rhode Island, or the 

criminal law of any other State during the period of this Deferred Sentence Agreement. 

2.  I will not leave the State of Rhode Island for more than seventy-two (72) hours without the 

permission of the court.  

 
3.  I will waive extradition from any State in the United States to the State of Rhode Island in 
the event that my presence in Rhode Island should be required by any court in this State during the 
period of this Deferred Sentence Agreement.  

4.  I  will  report to  the  court  as  directed for periodic reviews regarding  the  status  of my  
compliance of all deferred sentence conditions.  The first such periodic review is scheduled for  
________________________________________________________.  

5.  I understand that if I fail to appear for my review scheduled by the court, a warrant may 

issue for my arrest. 

6.  I will keep the court informed of my whereabouts and will notify the court within seventy-
two (72) hours of any change of my residential and/or mailing  address or place of employment. 

7.  I will make all payments of monies as directed by the court. 

8.  I will make every effort to keep myself steadily employed.  

9.  I will execute any necessary releases with treatment providers to allow the court to receive 

reports to monitor my progress with any counseling required under this agreement.

§ 12-1.5-2, who pleads guilty or nolo contendere, or is convicted of any felony shall have a DNA
