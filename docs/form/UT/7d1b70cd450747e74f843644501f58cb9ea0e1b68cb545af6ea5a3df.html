In this document: A judgment creditor is a person or company with a court order saying money is owed to them. A judgment debtor is the person or company who owes the money. A writ of garnishment is an order that property now belonging to the judgment debtor b ut held by someone else be used to pay the money owed to the judgment creditor. Interrogatories are written questions which identify the property and the value of the property that might be used to pay the debt.
