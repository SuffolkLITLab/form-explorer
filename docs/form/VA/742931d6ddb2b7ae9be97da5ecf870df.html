NOTICE TO APPELLANT: The notice of appeal must be filed with the clerk of the trial court and a copy must 
be transmitted to the Clerk of the Court of Appeals of Virginia and, except as otherwise provided by law, must be 
accompanied by the $50.00 filing fee required by Va. Code § 17.1-418. The fee is due at the time the Notice of 
Appeal  is  presented.  The  Clerk  of  the  Court  of  Appeals  of  Virginia  will  file  any  notice  of  appeal  that  is  not 
accompanied by such fee, but if the fee, or evidence that the appellant is entitled to be exempt from the payment 
of the fee, is not received by the clerk within 10 days, the notice of appeal will be dismissed.
