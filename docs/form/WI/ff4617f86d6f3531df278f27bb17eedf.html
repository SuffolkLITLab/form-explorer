aiver of Rights 

I understand that by entering this plea, I give up the following rights: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
I understand the rights that have been checked and give them up of my own free will. 

I give up my right to a court trial in a JIPS case or a jury trial in a CHIPS case. 
I give up my right to remain silent and I understand that silence of any party may be relevant. 
I give up my right to present evidence at trial. 
I give up my right to use subpoenas to require witnesses to come to court and testify for me at trial. 
I give up my right to confront in court the people who testify against me and cross-examine them. 
I give up my right to have the allegations in the petition proved by clear and convincing evidence.
