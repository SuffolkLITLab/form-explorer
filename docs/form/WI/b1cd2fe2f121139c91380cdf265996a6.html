TO THE JUVENILE:    
RAU TUS MENYUAM: 

You have the right to seek relief from the judgment in this case.  If you were represented by a lawyer at your 
disposition, it is that lawyer’s responsibility to assist you in deciding whether to seek postdisposition relief. 

Koj muaj cai los nrhiav kev cawm ntawm txoj kev txiav txim zaum no.  Yog hais tias muaj ib tug kws lij choj tuaj sawv cev rau koj 
thaum lub sijhawm txiav txim, yog tus kws lij choj lub luag haujlwm los pab koj txiav txim siab saib puas tsim nyog nrhiav kev 
cawm tom qab twb txiav txim tag lawm. 

If you decide to seek postdisposition relief, you or your lawyer must file a Notice of Intent to Seek Postdisposition Relief. 
The Notice of Intent must be filed in the trial court within 20 days.  If you had a lawyer at your disposition, and if you 
give your lawyer timely notice that you have decided to seek postdisposition relief, it is your lawyer’s duty to see that 
your Notice of Intent is properly filed in this court and served on the District Attorney. 

Yog hais tias koj txiav txim siab nrhiav kev cawm tom qab twb txiav txim tag lawm, koj lossis koj tus kws lij choj yuav tsum xa 
daim ntawv muaj npe hu ua Notice of Intent to Seek Postdisposition Relief. Daim ntawv Notice of Intent yuav tsum xa tuaj rau lub 
tsev hais plaub tsis pub dhau 20 hnub.  Yog hais tias koj muaj ib tug kws lij choj tuaj sawv cev rau koj thaum los txiav txim zaum 
kawg, thiab koj yeej ceeb toom rau tus kws lij choj ntawv yam muab sijhawm txaus es qhia rau nws hais tias koj tau los txiav txim 
siab nrhiav kev cawm tom qab twb txiav txim tag lawm ces, yog tus kws lij choj lub luag haujlwm los soj kom daim ntawv Notice of 
Intent raug xa tuaj rau ntawm lub tsev hais plaub no thiab xa ib daim mus rau tus Kws Lij Choj Hauv Zos no. 

After filing the Notice of Intent, your lawyer does not have to represent you further unless you hire him or her to do so. If 
you intend to seek postdisposition relief but cannot afford a lawyer, you have the right to request that a lawyer be 
appointed to assist you by the State Public Defender. 

Tom qab koj xa daim ntawv Notice of Intent, koj tus kws lij choj tsis tas tuaj sawv cev rau koj lawm yuav tsum yog koj rov qab 
ntiav nws tuaj xwb.  Yog hais tias koj xav nrhiav kev cawm tom qab twb txiav txim tag lawm tab sis koj tsis muaj nyiaj ntiav tus 
kws lij choj, koj muaj cai thov kom tau ib tug kws lij choj los pab koj dawb uas yog tuaj ntawm State Public Defender tuaj. 

JUVENILE’S STATEMENT: 
MENYUAM COV LUS TEEV: 

I have discussed my right to seek postdisposition relief with my lawyer, if any, who represented me at disposition.  I 
understand that if I intend to seek postdisposition relief, I must file a Notice of Intent in the trial court within 20 days after 
disposition and send a copy to the District Attorney.  If I want my lawyer to file the Notice of Intent for me, I must timely 
inform my lawyer of my decision to seek postdisposition relief.  I have received a copy of this Notice. 

Kuv twb tau sab laj txog txoj kev nrhiav kev cawm tom qab twb txiav txim tag nrog rau kuv tus kws lij choj lawm, yog muaj, tus uas 
tuaj sawv cev rau kuv thaum los txiav txim ntawv.  Kuv nkag siab zoo hais tias yog kuv xav nrhiav kev cawm tom qab twb txiav 
txim tag lawm, kuv yuav tsum xa daim ntawv Notice of Intent tuaj rau lub tsev hais plaub tsis pub dhau 20 hnub tom qab kev txiav 
txim tag thiab xa ib daim qauv mus rau tus Kws Lij Choj Hauv Zos.  Yog hais kuv xav kom kuv tus kws lij choj pab xa daim ntawv 
no, kuv yuav tsum ceeb toom rau nws thiab muab sijhawm kom txaus txog kuv txoj kev txiav txim siab nrhiav kev cawm tom qab 
qhov kev txiav txim zaum kawg no.  Kuv tau ib daim qauv ntawm daim Ntawv Ceeb Toom no.
