Alaska Court System 

Magistrate Judge Supplemental Application  
 Instructions 

The  Alaska  Court  System  will  consider  this  application  only  if  it  is  completed  in  full. 
Please check to see that you have answered each question. 

Please use the space provided for answers.  The Word Template version of the form will 
expand  to  accommodate  your  responses.    If  you  are  hand-writing  or  using  the  PDF 
version  of  the  form,  and  you  need  more  space  to  complete  your  answer, write the 
additional  information  on  appropriately  numbered  sheets  and  attach  them  to  the  main 
form.  For Section I responses, you may also provide additional details in question 11. 

This  application  constitutes  a  public  record  within  the  meaning  of  AS  40.25.110  and 
39.25.080.    All  information, except that which is specifically denoted as confidential in 
Section II, is available for public inspection pursuant to Administrative Rule 37.5(b)(1).  

The  Alaska Court System  asks that each applicant  review the Code of Judicial Conduct 
(Alaska  Rules  of  Court).    Please  note  that  Ethics  Opinion  No.  85-1  of  the  Alaska  Bar 
Association provides that the proscription of Canon 5 (formerly Canon 7) of the Code of 
Judicial Conduct specifically applies to candidates for appointment to judicial office under 
Alaska's merit selection plan. 

Magistrate judges are appointed to a four-year term and serve a one-year probationary 
period.  Following successful completion of the probationary period, an incumbent may 
only  be  removed  from  office  for  cause.    A  magistrate  judge  may  be  reappointed  to 
additional four-year terms. 

Contact the Human Resources (HR) Department or the Area Court Administrator if you 
have questions. 

Please return this supplemental application to the HR Department: 

By mail to 820 W. 4th Avenue, Anchorage, AK 99501 
By email to recruitment@akcourts.gov
