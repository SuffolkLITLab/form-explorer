IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED that  
 is  appointed 
guardian ad litem (GAL) for the minor in this matter.  The GAL shall investigate by interviewing 
the  minor  and  persons  having  substantial  knowledge  of  the  minor;  determine  the  need  for 
medical or physical evaluation of the  minor; attend  hearings  and  offer testimony  or evidence; 
and perform all other duties required of a GAL by law or court rule.  The GAL shall be prepared 
to give recommendations to the court based upon his or her own observations combined with 
the  information  from  other  sources  as  to  what  course  of  action  is  in  the  best  interest  of  the 
minor. 

CONFIDENTIAL  RECORDS:    This  order  shall  constitute  a  court-ordered  release  of  information 
allowing the GAL access, without further court order, to confidential and privileged records and 
information  pertaining  to  the  minor.  The  GAL  is  authorized  access  to  educational  records, 
medical/psychiatric/psychological  records,  court  records, 
justice  records,  child 
protection records,  and financial records  maintained  on the  minor.  This order shall constitute 
the authority for officials in possession of such records and information to discuss their concerns 
regarding the minor with the GAL.
