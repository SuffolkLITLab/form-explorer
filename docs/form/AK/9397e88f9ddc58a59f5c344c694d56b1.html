VICTIM RESTITUTION INFORMATION 
(Confidential – For Use Only by the Court) 

The  purpose  of  this  form  is  to  provide  confidential  victim  information  to  the  Court  to  enable 
restitution payments to be made to the victims.  Do NOT serve this form on the defendant or 
defendant’s attorney.   

1.

2.

3.

The prosecutor must file this form with a proposed restitution judgment.  The court will
not accept a proposed judgment without this form.

If a restitution judgment has already been entered, the prosecutor must submit this
form within 30 days.

If  no  district  attorney  participated  in  the  case,  the  clerk  must  complete  this  form  and
forward  it  to  the  Fiscal  Operations  Restitution  Unit  with  a  copy  of  the  restitution
order/judgment.

Clerk’s Instructions:  Immediately email this form to the Restitution Unit at 
restitution@akcourts.us.  Destroy this document after emailing it.
