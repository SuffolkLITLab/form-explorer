How Can Library Staff Help? 

he Alaska State Court Law Library serves 

the legal research needs of all Alaskans. 
Library staff members are available to assist 
you in Anchorage, Fairbanks, and Juneau.  

ibrary Staff can: 

▪  Provide help with using the library’s 

public computers, copiers, and catalog 
▪  Explain how to use legal research tools 

and databases 

▪  Help you locate a particular statute or 
case when you have a reference or 
citation 

▪  Recommend legal resources 
▪  Suggest a research strategy 
▪  Assist with legislative history research 

ibrary Staff cannot:
