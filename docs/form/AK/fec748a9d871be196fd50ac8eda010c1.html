Family Law

website at: www.courts.alaska.gov/shc/family/selfhelp.htm.   Or call (907) 
264-0851 in Anchorage and outside Alaska, or (866) 279-0851 (toll free in 
Alaska but outside of Anchorage).  A facilitator can explain court procedure 
and give you forms for each part of the case, but cannot give legal advice.
