QUALIFIED PROTECTED PERSON.
The protected person is qualified for protection against financial abuse because:
  The protected person is an adult who cannot effectively manage his or her 
property and affairs, and who has property that will be wasted or dissipated 
unless proper management is provided. [AS 13.26.401(2)]

PROBABLE CAUSE.

1.

Robbery, extortion, coercion under AS 11.41.500 to AS 11.41.530.

Offenses against property under AS 11.46.100 to AS 11.46.740.

Exploiting the protected person or the person’s resources for personal
gain, where there is no significant benefit to the protected person.
