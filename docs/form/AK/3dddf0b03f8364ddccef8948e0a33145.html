INFORMATION ABOUT CSSD 
December 6, 2002 
 
The  Child  Support  Services  Division  (CSSD)  is  the  state  agency  responsible  for  enforcing  child 
support  orders.    In  order  for  CSSD  to  enforce  a  support  order,  a  parent  must  apply  for  CSSD 
services.  Or, if the custodial parent is receiving public assistance, or the child is in state custody, 
CSSD will open a case automatically.  

SSD  can  establish  administrative  support  orders,  and  modify  existing  orders  when  there  is  a 

change in the income of the paying parent.
