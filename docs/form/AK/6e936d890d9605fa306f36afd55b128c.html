Alaska Court System 
Eviction Diversion Program 
Pre-Filing Information Sheet

Your landlord has given you a Notice to Quit. This is the first step in the eviction process. 
If you and your landlord do not work things out, your landlord may start a court case to evict you. 

A Notice to Quit tells you:

The reason the landlord wants to evict you,
A way to fix the problem, if it can be fixed, and
The day you need to move out by to stop the landlord from starting an eviction case. 

Act

Do not ignore the Notice to Quit. 

If you ignore the notice to quit, your landlord may start a court case to evict you. 

You have options:

Try to fix the problem,

Talk to your landlord and try to work out an agreement, or
You move out.
