Note: The interest rate on a particular judgment does not change, even though it may take several years 
for the creditor to pay it in full.  For example: The interest rate on a judgment entered in 2019 is 6%, 
and it will stay 6% until the judgment is paid.  It is not affected by the fact that new judgments entered 
in later years will have a different interest rate.

2   Between July 1, 1980 and August 7, 1997, rates were 10.5% (as long as the complaint was filed

3   Pre-judgment interest starts (or “accrues”) when you could first sue a person about it.  Examples

breached.  Post-judgment interest starts on the date the judge signs the judgment.

HOW TO DETERMINE PRE- & POST-JUDGMENT INTEREST RATES 

AS 09.30.070(a) 

How to Determine Pre- & Post-Judgment Interest Rates 

Is there a contract that sets the interest rate?   
If yes, the interest rate is what the contract says.  If no, go to #2. 

Is there a statute other than AS 09.30.070 that sets the interest rate?1    
If yes, use the interest rate required by the statute.  If no, go to #3.
