Instructions for Completing the Affidavit of Mailing Published Notice 

HEADING 

a. Choose the county in the drop down box below the first blank. This is where you

filed the Complaint or Petition.

b. Check the box next to what kind of case this is

NOTE: These checkboxes and names will NOT print on your completed form.

i.

In cases for Divorce:

1. Enter your name. You are the plaintiff.
2. Enter your spouse’s name. Your spouse is the defendant.

ii.

In Cases for Name Change for a Minor:

1. Enter the current names of the child(ren) that you are requesting

name changes for in this case.

2. Enter your name as “Parent or Guardian”

iii.

In Cases for Name Change for an Adult:

1. Enter your current name You are the Petitioner.

iv.

In cases for Emancipation:

1. Enter your name You are the Petitioner.
