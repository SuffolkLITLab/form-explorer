and willful course of conduct directed at a specific person which seriously terrifies, 
threatens, or intimidates the person and which serves no legitimate purpose.  Course of 
conduct means a pattern of conduct composed of a series of acts over a period of time, 
however short, evidencing a continuity of purpose, including a series of acts of following,
