Fair Credit Reporting Act Disclosure Statement 

The Federal Fair Credit  Reporting  Act, as amended  by the Consumer Credit Reporting  Reform 
Act of 1996, permits the procurement of consumer credit reports for employment  purposes. 

The  Act  provides  that  a  clear  and  conspicuous  disclosure  shall  be  made  in  writing  to  the 
applicant  before  the  report  is  procured,  that  a  consumer  report  may  be  obtained  for 
admission/reinstatement purposes and the applicant shall authorize the procurement of the report 
in  writing.  In  using  the  report  for  admission/reinstatement  purposes,  before  taking  any  adverse 
action based in whole or in part on the report, the subscriber shall provide the applicant a copy of 
the report and a copy of "Summary of Consumer Rights".  The information from the report will 
not be used  in violation of any applicable federal or state equal employment  law or regulation. 

The Nebraska State Bar Commission has contracted with Experian to procure a consumer  credit 
report  on  each  admissions  applicant and/or reinstatement applicant.  A  copy  of  the  applicant's 
report,  instructions  to  the  report,  and  "Summary  of  Consumer  Rights"  document  will  be 
mailed  to  the  home  address  of  the  applicant  upon  request.  The  consumer  credit report  is  also 
available through the applicantâ€™s admissions application in the Applicant Documentation section 
of the application.  A copy of the applicant's consumer credit report and instructions to the report 
will  be  included  in  the  materials  provided  to  the  Interim  Director  of  the  Attorney  Services 
Division.  If  the  applicant's  file  is  submitted  to  the  Commission  for  review,  a  copy  of  the 
consumer credit report will be forwarded to the Nebraska State Bar Commission.  Any corrective 
documentation  of  the  consumer  credit  report  will  be  provided  to  the  Interim  Director  and  if 
applicable, to the Commission members. 

I hereby  authorize the  Nebraska  State Bar  Commission  to  obtain a  copy  of my  consumer  credit 
report for admission/reinstatement purposes.
